21. define the ff academic text 1. article 2.conference paper 3. reviews 4. thesis and dissertion


Article
-a piece of writing included with others in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication.
"an article about middle-aged executives"
synonyms: report, account, story, write-up, feature, item, piece, column, review, commentary
"an article in the paper"

Conference Paper
-refer to articles that are written with the goal of being accepted to a conference: typically an annual (or biannual) venue with a specific scope where you can present your results to the community, usually as an oral presentation, a poster presentation, or a tabled discussion.

Review
-a formal assessment or examination of something with the possibility or intention of instituting change if necessary.
"a comprehensive review of defense policy"
synonyms: analysis, evaluation, assessment, appraisal, examination, investigation, inquiry, probe, inspection, study
"the council undertook a review"

Thesis
-a formal assessment or examination of something with the possibility or intention of instituting change if necessary.
"a comprehensive review of defense policy"
synonyms: analysis, evaluation, assessment, appraisal, examination, investigation, inquiry, probe, inspection, study
"the council undertook a review"

Dissertion
-a long essay on a particular subject, especially one written as a requirement for the Doctor of Philosophy degree.
"Joe wrote his doctoral dissertation on Thucydides"
synonyms: essay, thesis, treatise, paper, study, discourse, disquisition, tract, monograph
"a dissertation on Hungarian folk music"
