29. What musical form has three sections and common in western music?​


Answer:

Ternary Form

Ternary form, in music, a form consisting of three sections, the third section normally either a literal or a varied repeat of the first.

Ternary form, sometime called song form, is a three-part musical form where the first section (A) is repeated after the second section (B) ends. It is usually schematized as  A-B-A.
