13. According to section 304.2 of nscp, the designated allowable foundation pressure of 75kpa


NSCP 2015

According to section 304.2 of NSCP, the designated allowable foundation pressure of 75 kilo-Pascal is for footing purposes which has a minimum footing width of 300 millimeters and a minimum depth of 300 millimeters into the natural grade.

Can an increase in size be allowed?

Yes. You can increase the size of the footing. However, there are some notes to take before increasing the size.The allowable increase is 20% for each additional 300 millimeters of width and/or depth. You can have a maximum value of three times the designated value only.
