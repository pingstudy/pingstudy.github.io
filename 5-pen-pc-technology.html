24. If 3 pcs of oranges cost P97.50, how much is 5 pcs of oranges?​


PROBLEM:

If 3 pieces of oranges cost ₱97.50, how much is 5 pieces of oranges?

ANSWER:

5 pieces of oranges cost ₱162.5

SOLUTION:

Divide 97.50 by three to get the cost of each oranges.

[tex]97.50 ÷ 3 = 32.5[/tex]

Now, each orange cost 32.5, multiply it by 5 to get the cost of 5 oranges.

[tex]32.5 × 5 = 162.5[/tex]
