Answer:

PROBLEM SOLVING

Jerry's grade in Mathematics for the first quarter is 95.75. His grade in the second quarter was 96.98 and 98.89 in the third quarter. If her average grade was 97.60, what is her grade in the fourth quarter?

[tex]\star[/tex]What is Asked? (Given Facts)

95.75 - Jerry's grade in Mathematics for First Quarter

96.98 - Jerry's grade in Mathematics for Second Quarter

98.89 - Jerry's grade in Mathematics for Third Quarter

97.60 - Jerry's grade in Mathematics for Fourth Quarter

[tex]\star[/tex]Operation to be Used

Addition and Division - we will gonna find the sum of all grades then we will gonna divide it based on the number of visible data.

[tex]\star[/tex]Number Sentence

[tex]\boxed{\sf{\frac{95.75 + 96.98 + 98.89 + 97.60}{no. of data} = n}}[/tex]

Solution[tex]\boxed{\sf{\frac{95.75 + 96.98 + 98.89 + 97.60}{no. of data} = n}}[/tex]

Since Apat na Yunit o Quarter ang hinahanapan natin ng average grade, ang magiging no. of data natin ay 4.

[tex]\sf{\frac{95.75 + 96.98 + 98.89 + 97.60}{4} = n}[/tex][tex]\sf{\frac{389.22}{4} = n}[/tex][tex]\sf{97.305 = n}[/tex]

Round off.

[tex]\boxed{\sf{97.31 = n}}[/tex]

Conclusion

21. Sheena's grade in Math for the first quarters are 92, 86,and 88. What grade she gets in the fourth quarter to get an average of 90?


Answer:94

Solution:

//To get the average, you have to add the scores then divide the total by the number of scores.//

To solve the problem, write an equation for it.

Let x be the grade Sheena has to get in the fourth quarter to get an average of 90.

[tex] \frac{92 + 86 + 88 + x}{4} = 90[/tex]

Solve for x.

[tex] \frac{92 + 86 + 88 + x}{4} = 90[/tex][tex] \frac{266 + x}{4} = 90[/tex][tex]266 + x = 360[/tex][tex]x = 360 - 266[/tex][tex]x = 94[/tex]

Sheena has to get a grade of 94 in the fourth quarter to get an average of 90.
