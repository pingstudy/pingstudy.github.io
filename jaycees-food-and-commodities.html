6. Jamie has 450 game cards, jaycee has the 128 game cards fewer than Jaime. how many game cards must Jaime give to jaycee so that jaycee will have 298 game cards more than he?


Jamie = 450 cards


Jaycee has the 128 game cards fewer than Jaime

Jaycee = 450 - 128 = 322 cards


Find the sum:

Total cards = 450 + 322 = 772 cards


Find the number of cards each should have:

Jamie = (772 - 298) ÷ 2 = 237

Jaycee = 237 + 298 = 535


Find the number of cards Jamie needs to give to Jaycee:

Cards to give away = 450 - 237 = 213

Answer:

A.

demand and supply

The equilibrium price is the price of a good or service when the supply of it is equal to the demand for it in the market. Thus the market equilibrium for a commodity is determined by balancing the forces of demand and supply for the commodity.
