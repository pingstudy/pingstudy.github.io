12. 550, 440, 352... 9th term?​


Answer: 3520

Step-by-step explanation:

First: Find the a1 or 1st term in 550, 440, 352.

a1= 550, a2= 440, a3=352

So, the 1st term or a1 is 550

Second: Find the common difference.

Solution:

d= a2-a1

d= 440-550

d= -110

Therefore, the common difference is -110

Third(last step): find the 9th term or a9 using the equation an=a1+(n-1)d or an=a1+d(n-1)

Final Solution: a1=550, d= -110, n=9

an=a1+d(n-1)

a9= 550+(-110)(9-1)

a9= 550-110(8)

a9= 440(8)

a9= 3520

Therefore, the 9th term or a9 of 550, 440, and 352 is 3520
