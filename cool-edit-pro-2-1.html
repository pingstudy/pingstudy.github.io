1. The first ten amendments to the Constitution, collectively known as the Bill of Rights, protect the people more than any other part of the Constitution. They establish such freedoms as: freedom of speech, of religion, of the press, and peaceful assembly; and freedom against unreasonable searches and seizures (i.e. without a warrant signed by a judge that shows probable cause); freedom against cruel and unusual punishment, that is, torture; and the right to a trial by jury.

Answer Adobe Photoshop

The currently best and commonly used editing program for raster images. The tools in Adobe Photoshop are much better when compared to GIMP. However, Adobe Photoshop is very expensive and has a monthly or annual license renewal.

GIMP

Is the same as Adobe Photoshop just lesser in performance but not that bad in performace. GIMP is a free and open-source software.

Blender

A free and open-source 3D computer graphics software. Blender is perfect for beginners in 3D CGIs before other more complex programs like 3D Max and Maya.

Snapseed

Is an application for editing photos on iOS and Android. Snapseed enables users to enhance photos and apply digital filters.  It is similar to the mobile version of Adobe Lightroom

Adobe Illustrator

Is used for editing Vector Graphics Image Software to create images that do not have pixels until rasterized.

PaintShop Pro

Is a raster and vector graphics editor. PaintShop Pro functions like Illustrator and Photoshop on a single software. The latest Corel PaintShop Pro 2020 has received good reviews. It is good for beginners, however, professionals find it lacking in extensive color management support and other more advanced technical features.

20. 1. what are the importance of photo editing?2.what are the basic concept of photo editing?3.what does the photo editing do?​


Answer:

1.Editing helps you get the best image possible, as close to what you imagined when you took the photo (or better perhaps). Niche photographers can hone and emphasize their personal style. And for businesses, editing helps cement branding. Photo editing is particularly important for ecommerce companies.

2.Photo editing (also called post-processing or postproduction) is the process in digital photography of making adjustments to photographs in a photo editing program. It's digital photography's version of a darkroom, where photos can be tweaked after a photography shoot.

3.Photo Editors take the imagery captured by Photographers and then review, edit, format, manipulate, and prepare for digital upload, use, launch, and sharing. A photographer may submit 20 images of the same topic, while the Photo Editor then selects the best image, or image(s) most appropriate for publishing.
