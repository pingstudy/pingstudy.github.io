What Are Irregularly Spelled Words?

Splitting the words enable students to read and spell words fluently and correctly as per phonetics. However, there are certain words in the English language that cannot be decoded properly and are known as irregular words.

Irregularly Spelled Words

Irregular words are phonetically irregular words that differ in sound from their spellings. These words often do not follow regular phonic or spelling rules. Let's just say, find a rhyming word with fed and led. You would definitely find difficulty in finding irregular rhyming words to spell. Irregular spelled words also do not follow the rhyming pronunciation technique.

For Instance:

Again, Earth, Said, Friendly, Been, Door, Because and so on.

Here the letter –ai produces –e sound in the words again and said thus proving that these are irregularly spelled words with different phonetics sounds.

Sight Irregularly Spelled Words

As Irregularly spelled words do not follow a letter-sound relationship, therefore Vaughn and Linan-Thompson provided a list of irregular words. Students cannot learn these spelling by pronouncing the phonetics and have to memorize them as a whole on the basis of sight. Mostly these words are used with high frequency in print materials and are easy to learn.
