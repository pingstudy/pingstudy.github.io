a. Which exerts the action force?

The girl's finger

b. Which exerts the reaction force?

The wall

c. How much force is exerted by the wall to the finger?

The force exerted by the wall to the finger will also be 50 N force but in opposite direction.

The third law of motion " For every action, there will always be an equal and opposite reaction". The girl pushes 50 N force and as the opposite reaction, the wall also exerted 50 N force in opposite direction to the force applied by the girl's finger.

In Newton's Third Law of motion, It states that for every action, there should be an equal and opposite reaction.
