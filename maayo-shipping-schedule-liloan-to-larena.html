3. How did the municipality of liloan got thier name LILOAN?


Answer:

Along its coastline, there is spot called Silot, where a whirlpool is created by the ebbs and flows of the waters from the bay. This phenomenon is called lilo in Cebuano. Because of this, the town was known as Liloan, meaning "a place where there is a lilo".

4. What is the best way to tell a caller that a product that was scheduled to ship on March 15 will not be available until April 1? The product will not be available until April 1 The product will no longer ship on march 15 The product should be available by April 1 The product will be available April 1?


The best way to tell the caller that an item won't be available until April 1 is to say "The product will not be available until April 1". By saying that, we will give clear information to the customer.

"The product will no longer ship on march 15" and "The product will be available on April 1” are probably good choices. But if we say that, the customer may be waiting and if there is a problem that causes the product to still not be available by April 1st, then that will make them dislike it even more.

The thing to note when telling the caller that his item that must be delivered by March 15th is delayed is to apologize in advance and tell him the reason why the product is not available in polite language. After saying "The product will not be available until April 1" don't forget to say that we will notify them as soon as possible when the item is available.

24. captain ali is the captain of the ship. the captain of the ship is captain ali. what is the name of the ship?


WHAT.

WHAT is the name of the ship.  

Or

The name of the ship is WHAT.

There is no other name mentioned in the riddle except for Ali and What. Ali is the captain and his name can’t be the same name of the ship. This riddle is usually recited orally as to confuse the guesser of what is really the answer since one can play with the intonation of the last line making it ambiguously asking or revealing. Since this is a riddle, the person being told must make a guess.  

In writing, you may intentionally put a period at the end, or just simply write it as a question. Either way is acceptable though some would argue once the answer is revealed.

Some also calls this as a catchy or catching question because it’s goal is to challenge someone to catch the hidden message.
