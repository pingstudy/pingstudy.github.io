30. get the average monthly consumption of the following electric meters. march 1020 kwh April 600 kwh may 900 kwh June 492 kwh​


Question:

get the average monthly consumption of the following electric meters. march 1020 kwh April 600 kwh may 900 kwh June 492 kwh​

Answer:

Given the monthly consumption:

March = 1020 kWh

April = 600 kWh

May = 900 kWh

June = 492 kWh

To get the average, add all the recorded monthly consumption divided by 4.

[tex]\sf Average=\frac{(1020+600+900+492)}{4} \\\\Average=\frac{3012}{4} \\\\Average= 753 \;kWh\\[/tex]

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{\sf The  average consumption is 753 kwh}}[/tex]
