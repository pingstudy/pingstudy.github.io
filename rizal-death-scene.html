8. Some artworks like Juan Luna's Spoliarium depicts a scene in a gladiator's death, while an image of the Mendiola massacre took by news journalists also captures the same feeling of dread and sorrow. Both the photograph and painting are examples of what historical material?


Photographs and paintings are historical materials in the form of images.

Historical materials, by definition, are items that can be taken as a source of history in the context of a period, an origin of a place and an event. It provides historians with the necessary information for them to write documentations based on those items.

In general, historical materials can consist of documents, oral traditions, music, etc. In the question, we are presented with Juan Luna's Spoliarium, which is an artwork/ painting. Also, the question also mentions an image of the Mendiola massacre that are taken by news journalists. Based on these two objects, we can conclude that both are historical materials in the form of images, since both come in the form of painting and photo. Historians have concluded and documented information based on these objects also.
