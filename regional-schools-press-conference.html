23. • Is it also right to disregard a question asked by a reporter in a press briefing/ conference? Why or why not?​


Answer and explanation:

It depends on the type of question being asked. If the question is repetitive, insulting, or even misleading, then it is better to politely disregard it. However, if the question is relevant to the press briefing, then it is not right to disregard it especially, if you have enough time to answer it. In summary, only answer relevant questions, and politely decline to answer useless, irrelevant, insulting and misleading questions.
