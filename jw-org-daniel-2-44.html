5. Kevin is 33 times as old as Daniel. 44 years ago, Kevin was 55 times as old as Daniel. How old is Kevin now?


Here is the answer and the steps in determining how old is Kevin now.

Answer:

Kevin is 3,564 years old now.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Based on the given information, determine the equations.

Let K = age of Kevin Let D = age of Daniel

2. Equation 1 will be K = 33D for "Kevin is 33 times as old as Daniel."

3. Equation 2 will be K - 44 = 55 × (D - 44) for "44 years ago, Kevin was 55 times as old as Daniel."

4. Substitute the value of K of equation 1 to equation 2.

33D - 44 = 55 × (D - 44)

5. After distributing 55 of the right side, resulting equation is 33D - 44 = 55D - 2,420.

6. Transpose similar terms. Resulting equation will be -22D = -2376.

7. Divide both sides by -22 to derive the value of D. D = 108 (age of Daniel)

8. Substitute this value of D to equation 1. K = 33 × 108

9. K = 3,564 age of Kevin

That is the answer and the steps in determining how old is Kevin now.

25. Ben is 3 times as old as Daniel and is also 4 years older than Daniel. How old is Daniel?


Algebraic expressions

Algebraic equations consist of several terms with mathematical operations. Mathematical statements can be expressed in algebraic equations. Solving the linear equation will give Daniel's age 2.

Further explanation

Algebraic expressions in mathematics are a combination of coefficients, numbers, variables, constants, and arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

The main composition of algebraic expressions are:  

1. phrases  

algebraic forms separated by arithmetic operations  

consists of one phrase (monomial) to many phrases (polynomial)  

2. variable  

is a value that can be changed, can be in the form of letters, for example, x, y, a, b, etc.  

3. constants  

is a fixed value, can be a number  

4. arithmetic operations ⇒ +, -,:, x  

Calculation operations include multiply, divide, add, subtract, etc.

Given

Ben is 3 times as old as Daniel

Ben also 4 years older than Daniel

Required

Daniel's age

Solution

We state in Algebraic expressions :

Daniel's age = x

Ben's age = 3x or 4 + x

So that we can make the linear equation :

3x = 4 + x ⇒combine like terms

3x - x = 4

2x = 4

x = 2

Daniel's age = 2

Ben's age = 3.2 = 6
