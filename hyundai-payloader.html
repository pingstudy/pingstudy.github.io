10. If a payloader will lift a pile of sand with a height of 5 m and a total work of 70 J, how much force is needed to do the task?​


The force needed is 14 N

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

d = 5 m

W = 70 J

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Force

Step 3: Solve for Force

Solution:

The formula for work is given by:

W = Fd

where: W - Work

F - force

d - distance

Substituting the value of W and d in the formula,

70 J = (F)(5 m)

F = (70 J)/(5 m)

F = 14 N
