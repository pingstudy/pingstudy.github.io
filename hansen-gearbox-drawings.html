1. what are the basic steps in changing the oil in gearbox?​


Answer:

1.Manual gearboxes usually require a gearbox oil change every 30,000 to 50,000 mi (48,000 to 80,000 km).

2.Automatic gearboxes usually have gear oil change intervals of between 60,000 to 100,000 mi (96,000 to 160,000 km).

3.It’s advisable that you change your gearbox oil filter at the same time as your oil.

4.If you have a gearbox leak repaired, then always change your oil after the repair to ensure it’s fully topped up.

3. the baker's neighbors by merrily P. Hansen​


Answer:

"The Baker's Neighbors" is a short story written by Merrily P. Hansen. It tells the story of a baker named Mr. Gruber who lived in a small village. Mr. Gruber was known for his delicious bread, cakes, and pastries, and the people in the village loved to buy his goods.

One day, Mr. Gruber decided to bake a special cake for his wife's birthday. He worked hard in his kitchen, mixing and stirring the ingredients, and soon the cake was ready to be put in the oven. As he was about to place the cake in the oven, he realized that he had run out of firewood.

Mr. Gruber went outside to gather some firewood, but he found that all the trees in his yard had been cut down. He became worried and wondered how he would bake the cake without firewood. Just then, he heard a knock on his door.

It was his neighbor, Mrs. Schmidt. She had heard about Mr. Gruber's problem and had brought him a bundle of firewood. Mr. Gruber was grateful for her kindness and immediately started the oven. The delicious aroma of the cake filled the air, and soon, Mrs. Schmidt's husband and other neighbors came to see what was happening.

Mr. Gruber invited them in and offered them some of the cake. They all enjoyed it immensely and praised Mr. Gruber's baking skills. From that day on, Mr. Gruber's neighbors started bringing him firewood whenever he needed it, and in return, he would bake them delicious treats.

The story teaches us about the importance of helping each other and being kind to our neighbors. It also shows how a small act of kindness can lead to a strong bond between neighbors and bring a community together.
