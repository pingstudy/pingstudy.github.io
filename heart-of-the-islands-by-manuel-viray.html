She used 3 3/4 kilograms in making leche flan

How many kilograms of sugar were left

Mrs. Viray bought 5 2/4 kilograms of sugar

Asked:

The kilograms of sugar were left.

Given:

5 2/4 kilograms of sugar was bought. 3 ¾ kilograms of it was used in making leche flan.

Operation:

Subtraction

Number Sentence:

5 2/4-3 ¾=n

Solution:

[tex]5 \: \frac{2}{4} - 3 \: \frac{3}{4} = n \\ n = 4 \: \frac{6}{4} - 3 \: \frac{3}{4} \\ n= 1 \: \frac{3}{4} [/tex]

Checking:

[tex]5 \: \frac{2}{4} - 1 \: \frac{3}{4} \\ = 4 \: \frac{6}{4} - 1 \: \frac{3}{4} \\ = 3 \: \frac{3}{4} [/tex]

Final Answer:

There were 1 ¾ kilograms of sugar left.
