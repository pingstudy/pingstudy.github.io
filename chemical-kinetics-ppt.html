10. Chemical kinetics is related to the speed of chemical reactions, as well as the factors that influence it. What factors that contains influence the speed of transformations? ​


CHEMICAL KINETICS

ANSWER:

The factors that influence the speed of chemical transformations are temperature, concentration and contact surface.

EXPLANATION:

About temperature: Increasing the temperature boosts the kinetic energy of the reactant particles, thus increasing the number of effective impacts and the speed of reaction.

That is, the higher the temperature, the faster the transformation.

About the concentration: As in the case of temperature, the increase in the concentration of the reagents also accelerates the speed of the reaction, that is, they are directly proportional magnitudes.

About the contact surface: The greater the DIRECT contact between the reagents, the faster the reaction will be. For this reason, an effervescent tablet dissolves faster when crushed than a whole one when placed in a glass of water: more molecules are in direct contact with the water when the tablet is crushed.
