Answer:

A. 3150

B. 14,112

Step-by-step explanation:

Out of 10 arithmetic problems he can select 2 in =10!/{(10–2)!(2!)}=45 waysOut of 8 geometry problems he can select 4 in =8!/(8–4)!(4!)}= 70 ways=45x70=3150ways


Out of 10 arithmetic problems he can select 5 in =10!/{(10–5)!(5!)}= 252 waysOut of 8 geometry problems he can select 3 in =8!/(8–3)!(3!)}= 56 ways=252x56=14,112ways
