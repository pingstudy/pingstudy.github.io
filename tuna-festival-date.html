14. religious icon/industry tuna festival​


Answer:

Tuna Festival is the only festival that successfully coined the tagline for Gensan as the “Tuna Capital of the Philippines”. To live up to the name, activities to honor the prime industry (fishing) of the city, remained an annual challenge for festival stakeholders and event organizers.

Through the years, the challenge to level up and introduce improvements in the celebration and sustain its promotion and marketing remained in our search for continuous uniqueness . Foremost among the changes, is the step to institutionalize Gensan Festivals. Second, the creation of a Festival Board, the body that approves the events included in the festival. Bottomline is to differentiate the Kalilangan from Tuna Festival events, and no duplication of activities therein.
