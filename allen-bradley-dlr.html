5. how does galileo, roemer, bradley and fizeau contributed to the eventual acceptance of the view that the speed of lights is finite?.


Answer:

Galileo's contribution was that he discovered 4 av Jupiters largest moons. Roemer discovered that the orbital times of Jupiters moons varied in a consistent manner, depending on the yearly change of distance between Earth and Jupiter. Through this variation, Römer could make a first approximation of the speed of lightHippolyte Fizeau determined the speed of light between an intense light source and a mirror about 8 km distant. The light source was interrupted by a rotating cogwheel with 720 notches that could be rotated at a variable speed of up to hundreds of times a second.

21. Allen was a pig at dinner.​


Answer:

Metaphor

Step-by-Step Explanation:

The sentence compares Allen to a pig, without literally meaning it.

If the sentence compared two things using the words 'as' or 'like', the sentence would be a simile.

Because the sentence compares two things without 'as' or 'like', the sentence is a metaphor.
