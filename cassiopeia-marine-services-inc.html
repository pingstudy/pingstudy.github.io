1. there are two services that is offered by the marine water ecosystem. the_______ services and the ________ services ​


Answer:

Ecological and Economic

Explanation:

The two services that are offered by the marine water ecosystem are the ecological services and the economic services. Ecological services are the benefits that humans and other organisms receive from the natural processes and functions of the marine environment, such as the production of oxygen, the regulation of climate and weather, and the protection of coastal areas from erosion and flooding. Economic services are the direct benefits that humans receive from the marine environment in the form of resources such as fish and other seafood, oil and natural gas, and minerals.

30. How did the constellation “Cassiopeia” get its name?


Question:

How did the constellation “Cassiopeia” get its name?

Answer:

The constellation “Cassiopeia” got its name from the Greek mythology. Cassiopeia was the queen of Ethiopia and wife of King Cepheus. She was known for her beauty and boasted that she was more beautiful than the sea nymphs, the Nereids. This angered the sea god Poseidon, who sent a sea monster, Cetus, to attack Ethiopia. To appease Poseidon, Cassiopeia offered her daughter, Andromeda, as a sacrifice to the sea monster. However, Andromeda was saved by the hero Perseus, who turned the sea monster to stone using the head of the Medusa.

After her death, Cassiopeia was immortalized as a constellation in the northern sky. The star pattern resembles a W or M shape, which is said to represent the queen’s throne or her shackled figure, as punishment for her arrogance.
