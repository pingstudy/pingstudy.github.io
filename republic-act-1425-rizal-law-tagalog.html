17. Why do we need to study about Republic Act No.1425 or Rizal Law? (Atleas 200 words)​


The Importance of Study About Republic Act No.1425 or Rizal Law

To appreciate the significance of Rizal's principles and teachings in light of the social trends and issues of the day. to promote the use of these ideas in solving current social and personal difficulties and issues. to gain a greater appreciation for and comprehension of all Rizal sacrificed his life for. It is crucial that students comprehend the justification for taking a college-level Rizal course. The Noli Me Tangere and the El Filibusterismo are incorporated into the Filipino topic for high school students as part of the general curriculum. But in the Philippines' colleges and universities, Rizal is a subject that is necessary for every course.

Republic Act 1425, often known as the Rizal Law, requires that Jose Rizal's life, works, and writings be taught in schools. The law's sponsor, Senator Jose P. Laurel, argued that since Rizal was the father of Philippine nationalism and made significant contributions to the country's current standing, it is only fitting that the country's youth and all citizens learn about and imbibe the lofty ideals for which he gave his life.

The Rizal Law, passed in 1956, aims to achieve the following objectives:

To recommit young people to the ideals of liberty and nationalism for which our heroes sacrificed their lives; To honor our national hero for dedicating his life and works to form the Filipino character; and To gain inspiration for patriotism by studying Rizal's life, works, and writings.
