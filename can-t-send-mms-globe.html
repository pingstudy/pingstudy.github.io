3. III. Direction: Solve. Show clear and neat solutions. 14. A giant globe is constructed in front of the school's Math garden. If it has a radius of 48 in what is its volume? 15. A can of milk is shaped like a cylinder. It has a radius of 60 mm and a height of 120 mm its volume.​


Answer:

14. 463246.69 in^3

15. 1357168.03 mm^3

Step-by-step explanation:

14. Volume of sphere = 4/3 *pi*r^3

Volume = 4/3 * pi * (48 in) ^3

Volume = 463246.69 in^3

15. Volume of cylinder = pi * r^2 * h

Volume = pi * (60mm)^2 *120 mm

Volume = 1357168.03 mm^3
