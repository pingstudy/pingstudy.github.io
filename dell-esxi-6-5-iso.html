Taco Bell is known for its classic items such as the Crunchy Taco and Doritos Locos Tacos, as well as its creative limited-time offerings. Del Taco, on the other hand, offers a wider variety of items such as burgers and fries in addition to their Mexican-inspired fare.

In terms of taste, both have their own distinctive flavor profiles. Taco Bell leans towards a more "Americanized" version of Mexican food with milder flavors, while Del Taco has a slightly spicier taste.

Ultimately, it's best to try both and see which one you prefer.
