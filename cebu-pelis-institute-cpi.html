12. explain yung formula on how to get CPI


Answer:

To find the CPI in any year, divide the cost of the market basket in year by the cost of the same market basket in the base year. The CPI in 1984 = $75/$75 x 100 = 100 The CPI is just an index value and it is indexed to 100 in the base year, in this case 1984
