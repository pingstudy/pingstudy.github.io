22. "Where do you think was the first mass in the Philippines held?" Support your answers with primary sources (e.g. individual persons, original documents, etc.) or secondary sources (articles, books, manuscripts, etc.)


Answer:

The first mass in the Philippines is believed to have been held in 1521 when Ferdinand Magellan and his crew celebrated a Catholic Mass at the beach of Limasawa Island in Leyte. This event is recorded in Antonio Pigafetta's diary, which is a primary source, as well as other accounts of the voyage. This event has been recognized by the Catholic Church, and commemorations are held in Limasawa each year. Secondary sources on the event include various textbooks, websites and news stories.
