25. Which of the following State Department officials are responsible for negotiations related to trade and business? A. ambassadors B. consulate diplomats C. embassy diplomats D. marines


The correct answer is letter C - Embassy Diplomats

Option A is not the correct answer because the ambassador functions as the representation of the state government on behalf of the president (https://brainly.ph/question/481567). He is responsible in treaty negotiations, immigration discussions, foreign aid projects and humanitarian aid projects.

Option B is not the correct answer because Consulate Diplomat or Consul is the person who is sent or commissioned by a particular country or state to foster the commercial affairs of the citizens of the sending state. More so, the Consul performs duties such as issuing visas (https://brainly.ph/question/2429574) to foreigners and renewing passport.

Option D is definitely not also the correct answer because the main function of a Marine is to combat in the ship and in the land. They are specialized in amphibious operations of a state. In other words they are focused more on state protection. This choice is the farthest choice among all other choices, hence, the first choice to be eliminated.

Option C is the CORRECT ANSWER because one the main functions of an Embassy Diplomat is to initiate and facilitate trade and commerce relations. In addition, Embassy Diplomats initiates and facilitates strategic agreements, treaties and conventions, and promotion of information.
