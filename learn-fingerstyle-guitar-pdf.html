What is a verb?

In grammar, a verb refers to a word or collection of words that describe or show action in different forms. Verbs show particular action (jump), happenstance or or occurrence (progress) or state of being (exist).

What is a subject?

A subject on the other hand, refers to the thing or person that is performing a particular action, the subject is the one being described or talked about, or the focus of the sentence.
