1. how long will it take 2500.00 double to 5000.00 when interest rate is 5% a month?a. 1monthb. 2monthsc. 3monthsd. 4months​


SOLUTION

F = PRT5000 = 2500(0.05)T5000 = 125T5000/125 = T40 = T

ANSWER

OPTION D (40 months)

Answer:

D. 4 Months

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]T= \dfrac{F}{PR}[/tex]

[tex] \\ T= \frac{5000}{(2500)(5\%)} [/tex]

[tex] \\ T= \frac{5000}{125} [/tex]

[tex] \\ T=40[/tex]
