29. The href is the attribute of _________ tag for location to be redirected. ​


Answer:

The HREF is an attribute of the anchor tag, which is also used to identify sections within a document.

The HREF contains two components: the URL, which is the actual link, and the clickable text that appears on the page, called the "anchor text."

30. what is the relationship betwen energy and hree laws of motion​


Newton's laws of motion are three physical laws that, together, laid the foundation for classical mechanics.

They describe the relationship between a body and the forces acting upon it, and its motion in response to those forces. More precisely, the first law defines the force qualitatively, the second law offers a quantitative measure of the force, and the third asserts that a single isolated force does not exist.
