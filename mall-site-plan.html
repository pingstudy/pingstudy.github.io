26. A group of college tudent are going to a lake houe for the weekend and plan on renting mall car and large car to make the trip. Each mall car can hold 4 people and each large car can hold 6 people. The tudent rented 4 time a many large car a mall car, which altogether can hold 56 people. Write a ytem of equation that could be ued to determine the number of mall car rented and the number of large car rented. Define the variable that you ue to write the ytem.


To determine the number of mall cars and large cars rented, we can set up a system of equations using the information provided. Let's define the following variables:

x: number of mall cars rented

y: number of large cars rented

We are told that the students rented 4 times as many large cars as mall cars, which means that y = 4x.

We are also told that the cars altogether can hold 56 people. Since each mall car can hold 4 people and each large car can hold 6 people, the total number of people that the cars can hold is 4x + 6y.

We can set up the system of equations as follows:

y = 4x

4x + 6y = 56

We can solve this system of equations to determine the number of mall cars and large cars rented.

The excavation will place parts of the mall’s foundation approximately 2 inches below the water table line. WHAT IS ASKED?

In the passage provided, locate the sentence that represents a preventive action to limit mass movement on the construction site.

ANSWER

"A construction company has been called in to divert all excess water flowing into the pond to an unused natural reserve 800 feet away."

The sentence clearly shows a preventive action to limit mass movement on the construction site.

Excess water significantly will make the soil saturated and saturated soil is prone to  movement. That is, the company's action diverting excess water is definitely a preventive action to limit mass movement in the construction site.
