30. what is archaic words


Archaic words are the words which used to be common in the past, but they're no longer often in present-day usage. They're still can be found in religious or historical books. Examples: thou (you), afore (before), aye (yes), ere (before), mayhaps (perhaps), methinks (I think), sooth (truth), twain (two), yede (to go) and many more.
