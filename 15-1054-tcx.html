11. How many numbers are there between 36 and 1054, which are exactly divisible by 2?


Answer:

508

Step-by-step explanation:

An = A + (n - 1) d

1052 = 38 + (n -1) 2

1052 = 38 + 2n - 2

2+ 1052 = 38 + 2n

1054 = 38 + 2n

-2n = 38 - 1054

-2n = -1016

-2n/2 = -1016/2

= 508
