12. tell something about the movie of thor ragnarok?


Title: THOR: RAGNAROK (2017)
Part of the Collection: Marvel Cinematic Universe
Rating: PG-13 (for intense sequences of sci-fi violence and action, and brief suggestive material)
Genre: Action & Adventure, Drama, Science Fiction & Fantasy
Directed By: Taika Waititi
Written By: Eric Pearson
In Theaters: Nov 3, 2017 Wide
Box Office: $211,589,707
Runtime: 130 minutes
Studio: Walt Disney Pictures


Personal Review:
There's no doubt Marvel's Thor makes me review my knowledge about Norse Mythology.  And maybe that's the reason why I love the movie. It's not just about comics, it is also referring to a different culture, literature and beliefs of other race.  I love the movie's style which critic of The Atlantic said, "Perfectly acceptable as an action movie but inspired as a comedy". 

I like how Thor and Loki still got together (or end up as a team) even if they are each other's nemesis: The God of Thunder and the God of Mischief.  But I can't also deny the fact that Cate Blanchett (as Hela) and Tessa Thompson (as Valkyrie) stole mega-attention from viewers because of their girl-power.  They did very well.

Same as the Marvel's fans, I was excited too to see Hulk. But I believe, Heimdall made a major part as well.  I think, Heimdall is the most realistic hero in this story. He is the character who has the closest similarity to the human's innate empathy and courage. 

Cliche it is but yes, it is a story with a moral lesson in the ending. It's like, the superhero movies must-have, so expect that.

This is one of the films that I will watch over and over again but not get sick. Well, because I'm a Marvel's fan as well as a mythology-junkie.
