19. 60 eggs to 5 trays xpress in ratio in simplest term using the fraction and colon form​


Answer:

The ratio of 60 eggs to 5 trays can be expressed in simplest terms as:

Fraction form:

60 eggs : 5 trays can be written as 12/1 eggs per tray (dividing both sides by 5)

Colon form:

60 eggs to 5 trays can be written as 12:1 (dividing both sides by the greatest common factor of 60 and 5, which is 5).
