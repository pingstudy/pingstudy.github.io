The statement is referring to the town of Lumban in the Philippines, which is famous for its production of embroidered piña fabric. Piña fabric is made from the fibers of the pineapple plant and is woven into a high-quality and costly fabric. Lumban is known for its intricate and beautiful embroidery work on piña fabric, which is used to create traditional Filipino clothing such as the barong tagalog.
