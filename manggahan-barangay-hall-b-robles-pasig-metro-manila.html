7. magkaiba ba ang manila at metro Manila? ​


Answer:

Manila vs. Metro Manila

Magkaiba ang Manila sa Metro Manila. Narito ang ilan sa mga pagkakaiba ng dalawang lugar na ito:

Ang Manila o Maynila sa Tagalog ay tumutukoy sa lungsod ng Maynila na naitatag noong ika-13 na siglo at napasailalim sa kapangyarihan ng mga Kastila noong 1571. Ang Metro Manila naman ay tumutukoy sa rehiyong National Capital Region, kung saan kabilang ang Maynila. Ang Metro Manila ay binubuo ng 16 na lungsod at isang bayan (Pateros). Naitatag ito noong Hunyo 2, 1978.

Sa kabila ng malaking pagkakaiba sa pagitan ng Manila at Metro Manila, marami pa rin ang mga nalilito kung ano nga ba ang pagkakaiba ng dalawa. Makati

Makati, formally the Metropolis of Makati, is a first-class highly urbanized city in the Philippines' National Capital Region.

Makati is the Philippines' financial capital, with the biggest concentration of global and local firms in the country. Makati is home to major banks, corporations, department stores, and foreign embassies. Before the Philippine Stock Exchange transferred its headquarters to Bonifacio Global City in Taguig, the city's Ayala Avenue was home to the stock exchange's largest trading floor. Makati is also recognized for being a significant cultural and entertainment hotspot in Metro Manila. It has a population of 629,616 people according to the 2020 census, making it the 17th most populous city in the country and the 41st most densely populated city in the world with 19,336 residents per square kilometer. Despite its small population, the city's daytime population is believed to be more than one million on a typical working day due to the enormous number of individuals that go to the city to work, shop, and do business.
