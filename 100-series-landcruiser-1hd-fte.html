1 hour + 0.75 hours = 1.75 hours

Next, we can use the formula:

average speed = total distance ÷ total time

Dawid rode his motorcycle 105 miles in 1.75 hours, so his average speed was:

105 miles ÷ 1.75 hours = 60 miles per hour
