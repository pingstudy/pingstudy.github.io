29. 4. Pipes A and B can fill the pool in 10 hours. Pipe A can fill the tank in 3x hours while pipe Bcan fill it in x hours. How long can pipe B fill the tank alone ?


Answer:

The pipe B can fill the tank in 40/3 hours or 13.33 hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

In mathematics, solving word problem required knowledge and skills in basic mathematics that will improve your analytical and critical thinking skills.

In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math specially in work problem.

ProblemHow long can pipe B fill the tank alone ?

Note: Work problem equation is 1/x + 1/y = 1/t, where x and y is the work of two person and t is time when they work together.

Givenpipe A = 3x hourspipe B = x hourspipe A and B = 10 hours

Solution

1/a + 1/b = 1/t

1/3x + 1/x = 1/10

30x(1/3x) + 30x(1/x) = 30x(1/10)

10 + 30 = 3x

40 = 3x

x = 40/3 or 13.33 hours

Thus, the pipe B can fill the tank in 40/3 hours or 13.33 hours.
