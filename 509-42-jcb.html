5. In which number does 7 have the greater value? 72 509 or 87 999


Answer:

72 509

Step-by-step explanation:

In 72 509, 7 is found in the ten thousands place. To get the value of 7, multiply it to its place value which is 10, 000. The product or its value is 70 000.

In 87 999, 7 is found in the thousands place. To get the value of 7, multiply it to its place value which is 1000. The product or its value is 7000.

It is very clear which one of the two (70 000 and 7000) is bigger than the other.70 000 &gt; 7000

Therefore, 7 has a greater value in 72 509 than 7 in 87 999.

[tex] \rm x^2 + (x + 1)^2 + (x + 2)^2 = 509 [/tex]
