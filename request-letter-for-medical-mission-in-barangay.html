I would like to request for a small plot of land where we can establish a community garden. It will serve as a platform for people to come and grow their vegetables, fruits and herbs which will be accessible to all residents. This will not only benefit the environment, but it will also promote healthy eating habits among our community.

Furthermore, a community garden will allow us to bring together our Barangay members and promote cooperation and teamwork among us. It will also give us an opportunity to enhance our socialization skills and develop a sense of pride in our community.

Step-by-step explanation:

25:6 = 20: __

Multiplying both sides by 20 cancels on the right

20 × (6/25) = (X/20) × 20

20 × (6/25) = X

Then solving for X

X = 20 × (6/25)

X = 4.8

Therefore

25 : 6 = 20 : 4.8
