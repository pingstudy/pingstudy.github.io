2. What is the sum of first 6 term of geometric sequence 12,24,48,96,...?a.756 b.856 c.956 d.1,056​


Answer:

A. 756

Step-by-step explanation:

12 is the first given number so...

12+12=24

24+24=48

48+48=96

96+96=192

192+192=384

you'll stop the geometric sequence at 384 because it's the first 6 term of the sequence.

12+24+46+96+192+384 = 756
