22. coach sweeney walks 26 yards to the north along the sideline, pauses, and walks 12 yards back to the south.Determine the distance which coach moved.​


The distance which coach moved : 38 yard

Further explanation  

Distance is a scalar quantity that indicates the length of the trajectory that is traveled by an object within a certain interval. Distance has no direction, only has a magnitude  

Can be simplified distance = totals traveled  

Displacement is a vector quantity that shows changes in the position of objects in a certain interval of time. Displacement has magnitude and direction  

Can be simplified displacement = distanced traveled from starting point to ending point  

Given

26 yards to the north

12 yards back to the south

Required

The distance

Solution

distance = totals traveled  

total traveled :

[tex]\tt =26~yard+12~yard\\\\=\boxed{\bold{38~yard}}[/tex]

different from the displacement(positive=north, negative=south)

= 26 yard (north) - 12 yard (south)

= 14 yard (north)
