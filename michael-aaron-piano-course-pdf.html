10. aaron is 5 years younger than ron. 4 years later ron will be twice as old as aaron. find their present age.


Answer:

Ron - 6 years old , Aaron - 1 year old

Step-by-step explanation:

Present year,

Ron = x

Aaron = x - 5

4 years later,

Ron = x + 4

Aaron = x - 1

Your equation: x + 4 = 2(x - 1)

Solving for x:

x + 4 = 2(x - 1)

x + 4 = 2x - 2

2x - x = 4 + 2

x = 6

So Ron will be 6 years old while Aaron is 1 year old.

27. Rachel is 10 years older than Aaron. after two years patient will be twice as old as Aaron. find the age of Rachel now


Answer: 18

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = Aaron's age now; y = Rachel's age now

y = x + 10

After 2 years. . .

y + 2 = 2(x + 2)

(x + 10) + 2 = 2(x + 2)

x + 12 = 2x + 4

12 - 4 = 2x - x

8 = x (Aaron's age now)

y = x + 10

y = 8 + 10

y = 18 // Rachel's age now
