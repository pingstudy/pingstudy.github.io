29. what is piano music and program music​


Answer:

program music=

Program music, instrumental music that carries some extramusical meaning, some “program” of literary idea, legend, scenic description, or personal drama. It is contrasted with so-called absolute, or abstract, music, in which artistic interest is supposedly confined to abstract constructions in sound.

30. what are the SIMILARITIES of program music and piano music?​


PROGRAM MUSIC AND PIANO MUSIC

Music that has an extra-musical theme to go with it is known as program music. It might be a word, an image, a tale, or an idea. There is no extra-musical thought to go along with absolute music. It's music for the sake of music, with no suggestion as to what it's about or what it's about.

The term "programme music" refers to a style of instrumental art music that aims to musically convey an extra-musical story. The audience could be given a sense of the story through the title of the piece or in the form of program notes, enabling them to make connections between the music and their own imaginations.
