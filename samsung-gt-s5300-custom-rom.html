27. samsung announced that they will have a sale on their phones. explain what will happen to the market of samsung phone cases.​


Once the sale on samsung phones starts, there is a possibility of high demand on samsung phone cases. We all know that samsung cases becomes in demand due to their use, for further protection of phones.

There is a great possibility that once the samsung phone price drops due to the sale, many people will buy them causing an increase in samsung phone users.

This increase of samsung phone users will also add up to the demand of phone cases. As an effect, its price has the tendency to increase applying the law of demand and supply. This indicates that when an item has a high demand in the market, there is a possibility for its price to increase.
