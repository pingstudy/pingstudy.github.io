8. which of the following is higher business?Insurance Companies VS power plants WHy?​


Answer:

It is difficult to compare insurance companies and power plants as they are vastly different types of businesses. However, in terms of revenue and profitability, power plants tend to have higher earning potential than insurance companies. This is because power plants generate and distribute electricity, which is a crucial commodity in our modern world, and their services are in high demand. Insurance companies, on the other hand, provide financial protection against risks and uncertainties, and their profitability depends on the number of policies they sell and the claims they have to pay out. While both industries play important roles in the economy, power plants tend to have higher revenue and profit margins due to the nature of their business.

As you get older, the price of life insurance usually increases. However, to determine your specific premium, insurers take into account other elements such as your general health, gender, the type of policy you choose, and the level of coverage you need.
