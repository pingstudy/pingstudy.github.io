Direction: Give the FIVE Rudiments of Dance Position.(2 pts each)

Answer:STEP 1:
A. Hand: Both arms are raised in a circle in front of the chest with the fingertips almost touching, with the back of the hand facing the front.
B. Feet: Heels close together, toes apart with an angle of about 45 degrees.

STEP 2:
A. Hand: Both are raised sideward with a graceful curve at shoulder level.
B. Feet: Feet apart sideways at shoulder width.

STEP 3:
A. Hand: One arm is raised in front as in 2nd position; another arm is raised upward.
B. Feet: Heel of one foot close to in-step of other foot.

STEP 4:
A. Hand: One arm is raised in front as in 1st position; another arm is raised overhead.
B. Feet: One foot in front of the other foot at a pace distance.

STEP 5:
A. Hand: Both arms raised overhead.
B. Feet:​ Heel of the front foot close to the big toe of the rear foot.

Answer:

Raden Adjeng Kartini, also known as Raden Ayu Kartini, was a prominent Indonesian activist who advocated for women's rights and female education. She was born into an aristocratic Javanese family in the Dutch East Indies.

Raden Adjeng Kartini is a Javanese leading figure and the national hero of Indonesia. Kartini is known as the pioneer of the resurgence of indigenous women.
