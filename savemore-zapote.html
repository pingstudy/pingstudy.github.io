4. Show your solution. 1. Las Piñas Engineering office installed electric posts along the stretch of Alabang-Zapote Road. They used 41 posts which were equally apart. How far apart is each post if the distance from Alabang to Zapote is 16.4 km? r, Michaela​


DIVISION: REAL-LIFE PROBLEM

Problem:

Las Piñas Engineering office installed electric posts along the stretch of Alabang-Zapote Road. They used 41 posts which were equally apart. How far apart is each post if the distance from Alabang to Zapote is 16.4 km?

Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

The required data for this word problem is the distance of two posts from each other. The given information are 16.4-kilometer distance from Alabang to Zapote and there were 41 electrical posts to be installed. The operation that we will use here is division.

By dividing 16.4 by 41, we will get a quotient of 0.4 km.

If 0.4 km will be converted to meters, just remember that 1 km is equivalent to 1,000 meters. Multiply 1,000 to 0.4, the result will become 400 meters

Therefore, each post is 0.4 km or 400 m away to the next post.
