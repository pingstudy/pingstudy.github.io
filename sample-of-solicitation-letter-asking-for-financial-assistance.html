2. Solicitation letter sample english for dance competition


If you need a fundraising for your upcoming event, big or small, some organizations or groups send solicitation letter to potential donors. Below is an example of a solicitation letter for a dance competition:


SAMPLE SOLICITATION LETTER 


Officer-in-Charge

XYZ Company


Dear Sir/Madam:


We are writing you on behalf of the ABC Talent Committee of Brgy. San Antonio, Paranaque. We are a committee formed to handle events in our Barangay, with members from the most creative talents of the homeowners.


This year, we are organizing a dance competition as part of the yearly event for the feast of St. Anthony every June 13th. We would like to seek assistance from your organization for a donation for the prizes. All collected amounts will be allocated to three (3) winners: 3rd placer, 2nd placer and the champion.


Prize donors will be acknowledged in the event program, and will be recognized in our newsletter and website. 


Your support will surely make this event successful. We would like to thank you, in advance, for your generosity. 



Respectfully,


Your Name

Position

Organization’s Name
