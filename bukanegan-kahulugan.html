8. ___ refers to the Ilocano balagtasan. Select one: a. Bukanegan b. Balitaw c. None of the choices d. Balagtas sa Balitaw


Answer: A - BUKANEGAN

Ilocano culture revolves around life rituals, festivities and oral history.

These were celebrated in songs (kankanta), dances (salsala), poems (dandaniw), riddles (burburtia), proverbs (pagsasao), literary verbal jousts called bucanegan (named after the writer Pedro Bucaneg, and is the equivalent of the Balagtasan.
