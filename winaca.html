11. Bianca is twice as old as pio and meggie is twice as old as bianca. in ten years, their combined ages will be 58. how old is bianca now?


Present Ages:
Pio: x
Bianca: 2x
Meggie: 2(2x) or 4x

The sum of their ages in 10 years will be 58:

(x+10) + (2x+10) + (4x+10) = 58
7x + 30 = 58
7x = 58 - 30
7x/7 = 28/7
x = 4   

Substitute 4 to x:

Piolo: x = 4 years old
Bianca: 2x = 2(4) = 8 years old
Meggie: 22x) = 2(8) = 16 years old

In ten years, their ages will be:
Piolo: 4 + 10 = 14 years old
Bianca: 8 + 10 = 18 years old
Meggie: 16 + 10 = 26 years old

14 + 18 + 26 = 58
58 = 58 (true)

ANSWER:  Bianca is 8 years old.

16. paano winakas ng may akda ang kwento ng ang ama


Answer:
