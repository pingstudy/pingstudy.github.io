14. 3. Illustrate how the C major scale is played in white keys of the piano​


Explanation:

Start with your thumb on Middle C, and then play the next 3 notes, D & E with your 2nd and 3rd fingers. Then, cross your thumb under your 3rd finger and place it on F, the next note in the scale. Continue with your 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th fingers consecutively until you play the 8th note of the scale which is C again.
