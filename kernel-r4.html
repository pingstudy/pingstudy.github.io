29. Kernel of the Atom..​


KERNEL OF THE ATOM

The part of the atom exclusive of its valence electrons. It also includes the nucleus and all the inner energy levels of the electrons.

Example: Aluminum Atom

1s² 2s² 2p⁶ (10 electrons) make up the kernel of the atom.

Neutral atom of Aluminum

[tex] \boxed{\tt \: AL \: (Z = 13)} \\ \boxed{ \tt \: 1s^{2} \: 2s^{2} \: 2p^{6} \: 3s^{2} \: 3p^{1} }[/tex]

At n=3, there are 2 electrons on the s sublevel and 1 electron on the p sublevel forming a total of 3 valence electrons.
