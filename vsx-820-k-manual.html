1. 6. Convert a temperature reading of 820 oF in kelvin (K).​


The answer is 710.928K.

Explanation:

Use the formula (°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15 = K.

Given:

Fahrenheit (F°) = 820

Find:

Kelvin (K) =?

Formula:

(°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15 = K

Solution:

(820-32) × 5/9 + 273.15 = K

788 × 5/9 + 273.15 = K

3940/9 + 273.15 = K

Convert 273.15 to fraction

3940/9 + 5463/20 = K

Make them a similar fraction

78800/180 + 49167/180 = K

Add

127967/180 = K

Convert it to decimal, then the value will be approximately...

710.928K

14. Find the smallest positive coterminal angle 1.−820°


Trigonometry

Find the smallest positive coterminal angle.

1. −820°

Coterminal angles are angles that have the same initial and terminal sides.

Keep adding 360 until you find the smallest positive coterminal angle.

[tex]-820° + 360° = -460°[/tex]

[tex]-460° + 360° = -100°[/tex]

[tex]-100° + 360° = 260°[/tex]

Therefore, the smallest positive coterminal angle of -820° is 260°.
