2. Ano ang ibig sabihin ng virtud


ANSWER:

Ang birtud ay katangian o kalidad na itinuturing na mahalaga sa moral ng isang tao at para sa pundasyon ng kanyang mga prinsipyo sa buhay.


3. ano ang pagkakaiba ng virtud at pagpapahalaga?​


Answer:

Ang pagpapahalaga ay kasanayan sa pagkilala sa mga pagpapala sa ating buhay at pagpapakita ng mga pasasalamat para sa mga ito. Kapag Nagpapahalaga tayo, natural na nagsasagawa kami ng iba pang mga birtud tulad ng Pasasalamat, Pagkakaalaala, Kabaitan at Pagrespeto.

Ang kabutihan/virtud ay kahusayan sa moral. Ang isang birtud ay isang ugali o kalidad na itinuturing na mabubuting moral at sa gayon ay pinahahalagahan bilang isang pundasyon ng prinsipyo at mabuting pagkatao. Ang mga personal na birtud ay mga katangiang pinahahalagahan bilang pagtataguyod ng sama at indibidwal na kadakilaan.

Answer:

1.)To inculcate the university's twin philosophy of ciencia y virtud science and virtue in my daily life, I would strive to integrate both aspects into my personal and academic pursuits. In terms of science, I would make an effort to stay updated with current research and technological advancements in my field of study. At the same time, I would also prioritize practicing virtuous behavior in my everyday interactions with others, such as being honest, respectful, and empathetic. By incorporating both science and virtue into my life, I can become a well-rounded individual who can contribute positively to society.

2.)As someone vying for the crown, I believe I can positively influence my fellow students in following the basic university rules and regulations by being a role model. I will make sure to abide by all the rules and regulations myself, and encourage others to do the same. Additionally, I would take an active role in educating my peers about the importance of following these rules, explaining how they contribute to maintaining order, discipline, and respect within the university community. I would also be approachable and willing to listen to any concerns or grievances my fellow students may have regarding the rules, and work towards finding solutions together.

3.)To contribute to ameliorating pollution, there are several simple ways that I can incorporate into my daily life. For instance, I can reduce my carbon footprint by walking, cycling, or taking public transportation instead of driving a car. I can also reduce waste by recycling, composting, and reducing my overall consumption of single-use plastics. Furthermore, I can educate myself and others about the harmful effects of pollution, and work towards advocating for policies that prioritize sustainability and the protection of the environment. By taking these small steps, I can contribute to creating a healthier and more sustainable future for all.
