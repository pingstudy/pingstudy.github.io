Disaster Supply Kit

It is very important for every individual to be fully prepared when a disaster hits the country. It will be very useful during the time of need. Every time there is a disaster, we tend to panic and lose concentration and senses as we only think of how to survive and go to a safe spot.  

Now, if you are prepared and fully educated on how to respond to a disaster, it would help you be in control and respond quickly. You can even help other people.

For the disaster supply kit it consists of the following:

Clean water that is inside a drinking bottle Flashlight with working batteries Matches and candles Prescription Medicines  Foods that do not need reheating or cooking and with longer expiration date.  Example would be: cup noodles, biscuits, canned goods, candies Small pouch where an extra money can fit Extra blanket and clean towels First Aid Kit such us alcohol, tissue, betadine, hydrogen peroxide, cotton, band aid Battery or power bank to be able to use with charging mobile phones Small radio with working batteries Extra clothes Whistle

These are all essential and could be a great help during a disaster. Always remember to focus on your survival and safety and be aware of the current situation you are at.  

For my answer to the question, the answer would be letter A – Make up kit. During a disaster, people would no longer focus on how they are going to look or what type of makeup that they should wear. It is an emergency type of situation and a make-up kit may somehow help like the nail clippers and other stuff but still it is not an essential part of a disaster supply kit.
