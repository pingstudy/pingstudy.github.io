21. do you think the president of the philippines should be a college graduate? why?​


I can provide some general points to consider regarding the qualifications of a president.

Having a college degree can be seen as a symbol of academic achievement and may indicate that the candidate has developed critical thinking skills and acquired knowledge in various fields. However, having a college degree does not necessarily guarantee that a person is fit to be a president. There are many other important qualities and skills that a president should possess, such as leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills, among others.

It is also important to consider that there have been successful presidents who did not have college degrees, such as Abraham Lincoln and Harry S. Truman in the United States. On the other hand, having a college degree may be necessary in certain countries where a president is required to have specific educational qualifications according to the constitution.

Ultimately, the qualifications of a president should be based on their ability to effectively lead and govern a country, rather than solely on their educational background.
