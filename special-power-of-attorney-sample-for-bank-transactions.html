8. Suppose you work in a bank/Agency and signed a confidentiality agreement regarding all their financial/official transactions, suddenly your best friend asked you the where about of your clients and the daily transactions of your agency/ bank. how would you deal with the transaction?​


Answer:

As someone who signed a confidentiality agreement, it is my ethical and legal obligation to protect the privacy and confidentiality of my clients' financial and official transactions. Therefore, I would politely inform my best friend that I cannot disclose any information regarding my clients or the daily transactions of my agency/bank due to the confidentiality agreement that I signed. I would also explain to my friend the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of financial transactions to protect the integrity of the banking system and the trust of our clients. It may be difficult to say no to a friend, but in this situation, I must prioritize my duty to my clients and my obligation to my employer.

A special power of attorney is a legal document that authorizes one person, called an agent or an attorney in fact, to act on behalf of another person, known as the principal, under specific, clearly laid-out circumstances.

18. 50 transactions using special journals​


Answer:

In contrast to a general journal, each special journal records transactions of a specific type, such as sales or purchases. For example, when a company purchases merchandise from a vendor, and then in turn sells the merchandise to a customer, the purchase is recorded in one journal and the sale is recorded in another.

28. Basic documents and transactions related to bank deposits


-A CHEQUE BOOK contains a document called a CHEQUE that instructs a bank to pay whatever amount is indicated. The cheque would have to be signed by the owner of the account in order for it to be valid.

-A DEPOSIT SLIP is a form used by somebody who wants to deposit a certain amount to an individual’s bank account. Deposits can be made in the form of a cheque of currency.

-A WITHDRAWAL SLIP is a document provided by the bank for anybody who needs to take-out a certain amount of currency from his or her account.

-A PASSBOOK is a little booklet that keeps a record of deposits and withdrawals made by the owner of the account. When a deposit is made by another person, the owner of the account would have to visit the bank in order to update the passbook so that it will reflect the transaction that took place.
