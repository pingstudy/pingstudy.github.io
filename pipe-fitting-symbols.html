17. procedure in gluing the pipe and fitting into one piece​


Answer:

Glue the Joints

Glue the Joints Make sure to coat the entire surface of both pieces. The primer will dry almost instantly. As soon as it does, apply a thin but complete coat of PVC solvent glue to both surfaces. Insert the conduit into the fitting and push until the conduit bottoms out in the fitting socket.

Prime the PVC Apply a thin layer of purple primer to the ends of your PVC fittings. Also apply the primer to the inside of the coupling. Let the primer dry for a few minutes [source: Maki]. Glue the new joint together After the primer is dry, coat the primed areas of the pipes and coupling with clear PVC cement.
