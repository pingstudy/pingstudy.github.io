Answer:

Rhizomes-a continuously growing horizontal underground stem which puts out lateral shoots and adventitious roots at intervals.

Runner-a person who smuggles specified goods into or out of a country or area.

Stolon-a creeping horizontal plant stem or runner that takes root at points along its length to form new plants.

Tuber-specialized storage stem of certain seed plants. Tubers are usually short and thickened and typically grow below the soil.

Bulb-A bulb is the glass part of an electric lamp, which gives out light when electricity passes through it.

Spatial Figures:spheresphereconecylinderpyramidcubeDefinitions:

A sphere is a geometrical object in three-dimensional space that is the surface of a ball.

A cone is a 3 - dimensional geometric shape that tapers smoothly from a flat base often but not necessarily round to a point called the apex or vertex.

A cylinder is a closed solid that has two parallel often circular bases connected by a curved surface.

A pyramid is a shape or structure with a polygon for its base and three or more triangles for its sides which meet to form the top.

A cube is a 3 - dimensional solid object bounded by six square faces, facets or sides, with three meeting at each vertex.
