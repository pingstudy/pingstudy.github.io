9. Jack made a loan of 23,500.00 from lending corporation that charges 8 % simple interest. How much must he pay after 24 months


Answer:

Jack must pay an amount of 27,260.00 after 24 months.

Step-by-step explanation:

P = 23,500 ; R = 8% or 0.08 ; T = 24 months or 2 yrs

I = PRT

= 23,500 x 0.08 x 2

= 3,760.00

final amnt = principal + interest

= 23,500.00 + 3,760.00

= 27,260.00
