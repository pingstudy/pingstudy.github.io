15. if TJ = 4x+20 and RK = 6x-50, how long is the diagonal of the rectangle?​


Answer:

We can use the Pythagorean theorem to find the length of the diagonal of the rectangle, which is also the hypotenuse of a right triangle formed by the sides TJ and RK.

The Pythagorean theorem states that in a right triangle, the square of the length of the hypotenuse (c) is equal to the sum of the squares of the lengths of the other two sides (a and b).

In this case, we have:

TJ = 4x + 20

RK = 6x - 50

Let d be the length of the diagonal. Then we have:

d^2 = TJ^2 + RK^2

Substituting the values of TJ and RK, we get:

d^2 = (4x + 20)^2 + (6x - 50)^2

Expanding and simplifying, we get:

d^2 = 16x^2 + 160x + 400 + 36x^2 - 600x + 2500

d^2 = 52x^2 - 440x + 2900

To find the value of x, we can set TJ and RK equal to each other and solve for x

Ano po ang chord ng ikaw ang ligaya ko by Ric Manrique

The answer is chord G,Em,C,D

Ricardo "Ric" Manrique Jr. was  a Kundiman singer in the Philippines.  He has been known as one of two Hari Ng Kundiman (Kings of Kundiman) in the Philippines, alongside Ruben Tagalog. He'd been a member  of the 1950s Mabuhay Singers.  He recorded his first album in the early 1960s,  and released with Villar Records most of his albums.  Manrique was best known for his songs,  which became successful Filipino movie theme songs.  The song entitled Ang Daigdig Ko'y Ikaw is the theme song from  the same-title 1965 movie that stars Fernando Poe Jr. and Susan Roces.  This was sung  alongside Pilita Corrales. Manrique died in California, United  States on 22 September 2017.
