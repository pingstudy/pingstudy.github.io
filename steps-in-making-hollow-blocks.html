22. Mang Rudy uses hollow blocks to make the house wall. Each hollow block is 6 2/3 inches high. If Mang Rudy makes a stack of 18 bricks, how tall is the wall in inches?


Answer:

The wall 120 inches tall

Step-by-step explanation:

To know how tall the wall is, you have to multiply the height of a hollow block by the number of hollow blocks being stacked.

[tex]6\frac{2}{3}*18\\=\frac{20}{3}*18\\=\frac{20(18)}{3}\\=\frac{20(6)(3)}{3}\\=20(6)\\=120[/tex]
