3. What is the percent crude protein content of a feed sample if on the analysis has 7% nitrogen?​


Answer:

The crude protein percentage (CP%) is calculated by multiplying the nitrogen (N) percent of feed by 6.25. This is because on average protein contains around 16% Nitrogen (1/0.16 = 6.25).
