Answer:

1. Update your graphics driver

Right-click Start button, and select Device Manager.

Check for graphics or video card drivers with either a yellow exclamation mark/ question mark/ down-arrow mark/ error notification.

If the driver has a yellow exclamation mark on it, double-click on it.

Open Properties.

Under Drivers tab, choose Update Driver.

Restart your PC.

New drivers usually bring various improvements, such as new features and improved stability, so you can see why it’s important to keep your drivers up to date.

This is especially important for graphics driver because newer versions can offer better performance for your screen.
