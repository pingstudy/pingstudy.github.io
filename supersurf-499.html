28. 27, 499 round to the nearest hundred is ?


Rounding - Off Numbers:

27, 499 when rounded to the nearest hundred is 27, 500.

Solutions:

The rule in rounding - off numbers to the nearest hundred is that:

if the digit on the tens place is equal to 5 or more than 5, add 1 to the digit on the hundred place and make each digits on the tens and ones places 0.

if the digit on the tens place is 4 and below, retain the digit on the hundred place and make each digits of the tens and ones places 0.

Thus, in the number 27, 499 since the digit on the tens place is 9, add 1 to 4 which is the digit on the hundreds place and make the digits of the tens and ones place 0. Therefore, 27, 499 will be 27, 500 when rounded to the nearest hundred.
