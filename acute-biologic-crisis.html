10. an acute angle has two acute angles​


Answer:

One acute angle will always measure between 0° and 90° . Two acute angles can sum to be either greater than, less than, or equal to a right angle. Two acute angles can be complementary angles (adding to 90° ). Two acute angles alone cannot sum to make a straight angle (180° ).

assessments or other markers of processes or outcomes that are drawn from bodily activity or other natural biological systems or events. Such measures include assessments of cardiopulmonary, endocrine, nervous-system, and immune-system activity.
