1. is the flamethrower have high heat bullets that burn any armored vehicles.


Answer:

No, flamethrowers do not have high heat bullets that can burn armored vehicles. Flamethrowers use a fuel-oxygen mix that is forced out of a nozzle and ignited, producing a jet of fire. This jet of fire is effective against soft targets like people, but it is not effective against armored vehicles.

A LPO lens (low power objective) has a magnification of 10x.

To calculate the total magnification of the microscope you have to multiply the power of they eyepiece and the power of the objective lens used.

[tex]\sf Total \ Magnification = 10x \cdot 10x[/tex]

[tex]\sf Total \ Magnification = \boxed{ \sf 100x}[/tex]

↬ Hence, the total magnification of the microscope is 100x.
