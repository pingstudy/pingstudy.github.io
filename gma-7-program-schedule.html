Books/Magazines - They hold Mars Ravelo’s character rights for filming and they also publish books based on their movies and their stars.

Streaming Service - iWant is a free streaming service that also has its own originals and movies provided by ABS-CBN

and a MOVIE STUDIO which is supposed to be used for an upcoming Darna movie.

They are also praised for their teleseryes such as Ang Probinsyano and Kadenang Ginto which are one of the Pinoy modern classics.

What does GMA have against this?

GMA is known for being the most reliable news network in the country due to the numerous awards that are accumulated by their journalists such as Jessica Soho and Atom Araullo. GMA News is also one of the most high-tech news facilities in the country. GMA is also the oldest of the two by 3 years but with it comes a very strong radio presence which they started as from the very beginning.

GMA-7 had a golden age back in the late 90s - early 2010s where they were the most notable and quite possibly the most popular TV network in the Philippines. Currently, GMA stands grateful and strong after the AlDub phenomena which rose to fame shunning Showtime back then.

In conclusion:

ABS-CBN is an essential choice for the typical viewer that enjoys drama, comedy, etc. GMA-7 is more reliant when it comes to news and they also have Eat Bulaga! which is a famous noontime show. ABS-CBN is what you would typically see in TVs of every Filipino nowadays but GMA is rather the same (only in some places).
