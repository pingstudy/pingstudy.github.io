25. A senior high school has 5000 students enrolled for the academic year 2018 - 2019. Of these, 1200 are majoring STEM, 1500 are ABM, 700 are HUMSS, and the remaining are GAS.What percent of the enrolles take hUMSS?What percent has choosen a major which is not STEM and ABM?What percent are enrolled to GAS?​


Answer:

1. 14% of the 5000 students enrolled are majoring in HUMSS

2. 46%

3. 32% are enrolled to GAS.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. 700/5000 = 0.14

0.14 is 14% in percentage.

2. In order to solve the answer here, we must first know what's the percentage of students are enrolled to GAS (Number 3 explanation)

so now that we have the 32% we will add the 14% in here.

32% + 14% = 46%

3. We need to know first how many students enrolled in GAS as it was not given there. We could get that number by adding all of the numbers then subtract to 5,000.

1,200 + 1,500 + 700 = 3,400

5,000 - 3,400 = 1,600

1,600 students had enrolled for GAS now divide that to 5000 students.

1,600/5000 = 0.32 = 32%
