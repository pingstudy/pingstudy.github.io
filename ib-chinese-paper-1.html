13. Give at least one belief of Chinese Buddhist if they hang the paper cutting inside the house..What will be the outcome in the lives of chinese ? ​


Answer:

WINDOW FLOWER

As paper became more affordable, paper-cutting became one of the most important types of Chinese folk art. Later, this art form spread to other parts of the world, with different regions adopting their own cultural styles. Because the cut-outs are often used to decorate doors and windows, they are sometimes referred to as 窗花; chuāng huā, "window flowers" or "window paper-cuts". These cut-paper decorations are often glued to the exterior of windows, so the light from the inside shines through the negative space of the cutout.[1] Usually, the artworks are made of red paper, as red is associated with festivities and happiness in Chinese culture, but other colours are also used. Normally cut-paper artwork is used on festivals such as Chinese New Year, weddings and childbirth, as cut-paper artwork is considered to symbolize luck and happiness.


14. chinese paper cutting elements and principles​


Ang sining ng paggupit ng papel (jiǎnzhǐ 剪纸) sa Tsina ay maaaring magmula noong ikalawang siglo CE, dahil ang papel ay naimbento ni Cai Lun sa Silangang Dinastiyang Han sa Tsina. Dahil naging mas abot-kaya ang papel, ang paggupit ng papel ay naging isa sa pinakamahalagang uri ng katutubong sining ng Tsino. Nang maglaon, kumalat ang form ng sining na ito sa iba pang mga bahagi ng mundo, na may iba't ibang mga rehiyon na gumagamit ng kanilang sariling mga istilo ng kultura. Dahil ang mga cut-out ay madalas na ginagamit upang palamutihan ang mga pintuan at bintana, minsan ay tinutukoy sila bilang chuāng huā (窗花), mga bulaklak sa bintana o mga paggupit ng papel sa bintana. Ang mga tao ay nakadikit ang mga papercut sa labas ng mga bintana, kaya't ang ilaw mula sa loob ay lumiwanag sa pamamagitan ng negatibong espasyo ng ginupit. [1] Karaniwan, ang mga likhang sining ay gawa sa pulang papel, dahil ang pula ay naiugnay sa kasiyahan at kaligayahan sa kulturang Tsino, ngunit ginamit din ang iba pang mga kulay. Karaniwan ang gawaing gawa sa paggupit ng papel ay ginagamit sa mga pagdiriwang tulad ng Spring Festival, kasal at panganganak. Ang mga papercut ay palaging sumasagisag sa kapalaran at kaligayahan.

A conceptual framework is a tool that researchers use to guide their inquiry; it is a set of ideas used to shape the research, a map that may include the research question, the literature review, methodology, and data analysis. Researchers use a conceptual framework to guide data collection and analysis. If, for example, the researcher wanted to discover whether males performed better than girls in a particular subject, he may look at the literature on the development of both sexes, as well as on the methods of socialization of boys and girls, as this could impact what subjects were of interest. The researcher would then study existing literature on male and female development and socialization to help explain what questions she should ask. Such as if girls are more interested in history when it involves actual people, whether guys prefer battle history, etc. The perspectives of boys and girls on a subject could influence their success in that area.
