27. !ARITHMETIC SEQUENCE!Find the sum of -50,+(-44),+(-38)...+2038+2044 solution please <3​


Answer:

S = 348,950

EXPLAINATION:

Find n;

An= a1 + (n-1) d

2044= -50 + (n-1) 6

2044= -50 + 6n-6

2044= -56+ 6n

2044+56= 6n

2100 = 6n

6 = 6

n= 350

Finding the sum;

Sn= n/2 (a1 + an)

Sn= 350/2 (-50 + 2044)

Sn= 175 (1994)

S350= 348, 950
