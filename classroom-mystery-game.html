2. What are the skills and attitude needed to solve the murder mystery game?


#BrainlyFast

The game is like a puzzle to solve who is the killer. The player will act being a detective and find out who committed the crime.

Keen observer

You should be able to observe carefully the surrounding for some evidence that will help you solve the crime. You look for some evidences like the weapon, the blood and possible traces for you to identify the suspect.

Critical Thinker

You should also be a critical thinker. Analyzing the evidence, the situation, the surrounding will help you figure out who is the suspect. One wrong decision will lead to wrong accusation and you failed the game.

Problem solver

You should also be a problem solver who will not get tired in solving the case and find out who the suspect is.

For me this three are the most important skills and attitude needed to solve the murder mystery game.

27. 28. Online courses that simulate the classroom onlinea. Podcastc. Online Gameb. Coursewared. Videos​


Online courses that simulate the classroom online is called Courseware.

Definition of Courseware

Courseware refers to software programs specifically designed for educational purposes. Originally available on CD as a course aid to instructors and to help them manage lectures, courseware has been revolutionized. Most are now available online with a variety of resources and supporting materials (reference books, research papers and journals).

Courseware is primarily used for online courses and certifications such as Microsoft Certified Professional courses.

Coverage in courseware

Courseware educational material studied with the help of a computer. It can be in the form of flash drives, compact disks or material placed online. Courseware can include:

Instructions on how to divide courses into lectures Notes to the instructor on leading a class Information for independent and computer-based learning Useful Web URLs for related materials
