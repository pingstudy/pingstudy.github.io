Question :

3. Find the surface area of spherical ball whose radius is 6 mm.

Choices :

a. 452.16 mm²

b. 425.61 mm²

c.245.16 mm²

d. 524.61 mm²

ANSWER AND SOLUTION :

Use this formula SA= 4 πr² to get the Surface Area.

SA = 4 πr²

= 4 × 3.14 ×( 6mm)²

= 4 × 3.14 × 36 mm²

= 452.16 mm²

✒️ Therefore, the surface area of the ball is A. 452.16 mm².
