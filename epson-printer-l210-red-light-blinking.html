10. There is a string of red and blue lights strung around a window. The red lights blink every 3 seconds, and the blue lights blink every 5 seconds. How many times will they blink at the same time in one minute? *


Answer:

The lights blue and red will blink 4 times at the same time in a minute.

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the least common multiple (LCM) of 3 and 5.

3 = 3, 6, 9, 12, 155 = 5, 10, 15

Multiply the numbers 3 and 5 since they are both prime numbers.

3 × 5 = 15

LCM = 15

Therefore, the string of red and blue lights blink at the same time every 15 seconds.

To find how many times they blink at the same time in 1 minute:

1 minute = 60 seconds

Divide seconds in a minute by 15 seconds:

60 seconds / 15 seconds = 4 times

The lights blue and red will blink 4 times at the same time in a minute.
