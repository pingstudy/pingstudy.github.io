HOLY OBEDIENCE TO THE WILL OF GOD

Answer:

There are times in our life when we recognize that obeying the will of God results in positive improvements, and that if we don't, major consequences can result. There could be a variety of motivating elements that keep us from obeying. I view holy obedience to the will of God by saying yes to the will of God when your heart says no and it is one of the most difficult things you can do in life. Whether it's a relationship, a career, or a life goal, God's plans for us aren't always what we expect.
