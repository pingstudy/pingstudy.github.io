Solution:

Per each turn, the circumference of a circle is

[tex]C=2\pi r[/tex]

[tex]C=2\pi (1.00\text{ cm})\\C=6.28\text{ cm/turn}=0.0628\text{ m/turn}[/tex]

There is 135 m of thread. So,

[tex]135\text{ m}\div0.0628\text{m/turn}=\bold{2149.68\:turns}[/tex]

26. how can you describe the two projection method used in pipe drawing ?​


Answer:

Two methods of projection used in pipe drawings are orthographic and isometric (pictorial). Orthographic projection is used to show multiple views of an object in a single plane. Isometric projection is used to show a three-dimensional view of an object in single plane.
