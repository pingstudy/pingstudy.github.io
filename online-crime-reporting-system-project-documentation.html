1. what is the electronic reporting system that facilitates crime documentation modernizes data storage and provide quick and reliable transmission of crime information from lower units of the pnp the national headquarters at camp crame quezon city​


Answer:

CIRAS

Explanation:

CIRAS is an electronic database system that facilitates crime documentation and systematic data storage and retrieval. The system presents a quick, fast and reliable transmission of crime information from lower police units and offices of the PNP to the National Headquarters at Camp Crame, Quezon City.
