Certification for Filing Action

The issuance of CFA is in proper action. This is because the opposing party refused or did not show up at the settlement venue or in the barangay despite the numerous invitations.

Here are the reasons:

The defendant refuses to show up for settlement after several invitations and proper heads-ups.

The Punong Barangay did not fail to notify both parties so they have the right to move it to the higher department for further action.

This is in accordance with Section 412 of the Local Government Code, which provides that disputes cannot be settled at the barangay level.

The opposing party may not want to settle the issue at the barangay level and since the rule has been followed already, proper procedures should be followed.

The Punong Barangay or officials cannot solve the case because of absenteeism and according to the law, if the barangay level cannot clear the case, the court proceedings should follow.

Usually, the role and responsibilities of barangay-level reconciliation are to fix minor issues and problems between parties but in worse cases, they are usually referred to the higher court.
