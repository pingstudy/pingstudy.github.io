Kaninong pangalan hinango ang Balagtasan?

Answer:

B. Francisco Balagtas

Explanation:

Hinango kay Francisco Balagtas ang pangalan na Balagtasan. Ang Balagtasan ay uri ng pagtatalo sa dalawang magkabilang panig na tungkol sa paksa ang "Balagtasan" ay hinango mula sa pangalan na Francisco Balagtas. Si Francisco Balagtas ay isang kilala na Pilipinong makata at may-akda, kilala din siyang Kiko o Kikong Balagtas at Prinsipe ng manunulang Tagalog. Natuto siya na sumulat at magbigkas ng tula kay Jose Dela Cruz na kilala bilang pinakabantog na isang makata sa Tondo.

Answer:

Explanation:

1.  V Isabelo de los Reyes  

-  prominent Filipino politician, writer, and labor activist in the 19th and 20th centuries. He was the original founder of the Iglesia Filipina Independiente, an independent Philippine national church.

2. Pedro Paterno  

- He was considered as  a traitor during the Filipino-American war as his interests are aligned in business, prosperity and money which are what the Americans are offering in exchange sovereignty.  

3. Jose Rizal  

- A multi-skilled National hero who was inspired by the deaths of the GOMBURZA priests.  

4. Andres Bonifacio  

- Andres Bonifacio lead the revolution against the Spaniards. He is also the supremo of the Katipunan.  

5. Emilio Jacinto

- He was a Filipino general during the Philippine Revolution and was known as the Brains of the Katipunan, the revolutionary society led by Andres Bonifacio. In his brief time, Jacinto helped to lead the battle for Filipino independence from Spain.
