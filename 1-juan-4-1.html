Juan walked1/3 km. while Marco walked 3/4 km. How many kilometers did Marco walk than Juan?

To solve you need to convert to a common base or denominator. a/a = 1, any number divided by itself equals 1, so we can multiply a a fraction b/c by a/a and the ratio of numerator to denominator remains the same.

The problem is asking for 3/4 - 1/3

Multiply the denominators 4*3 = 12 to get a common base.

Multiply 3/4 by 3/3 to get 9/12.

Multiply 1/3 by 4/4 to get 4/12.

14. mang Juan harvested coconut in their farm to make wine.(tuba) he filled his big jar with 10 1/2 gallons of coconut fruit juice. ga consumed 4 1/4 gallons and gave 3 1/4 gallons to his friend how many gallons of coconut fruit juice did mang Juan has left?​


Answer:

3 gallons

Step-by-step explanation:

Mang Juan had 10 1/2 gallons of coconut fruit juice.

Ga consumed 4 1/4 gallons, so the amount of juice left is:

10 1/2 - 4 1/4 = 6 1/4 gallons

Ga also gave 3 1/4 gallons to his friend, so the final amount of juice left is:

6 1/4 - 3 1/4 = 3 gallons

Therefore, Mang Juan has 3 gallons of coconut fruit juice left.

16. Juan borrowed P5,000 with 4% interest annually. How much is the interest if he will pay it for 1 year? P100​


Answer:

A = $5,200.00

I = A - P = $200.00

Equation:

A = P(1 + rt)

Calculation:

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 4%/100 = 0.04 per year.

Solving our equation:

A = 5000(1 + (0.04 × 1)) = 5200

A = $5,200.00

The total amount accrued, principal plus interest, from simple interest on a principal of $5,000.00 at a rate of 4% per year for 1 years is $5,200.00.
