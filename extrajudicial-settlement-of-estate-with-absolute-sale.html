24. 3. A real estate agent receive 3% commission on the first P 2,500,000 of a sale and 5% of the rest. How much commission is received on the sale of P 7,500,000 property?help Po​


Answer:

The commission received on the sale of P 7,500,000 property is P 337,500.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the commission received on the sale of a P 7,500,000 property, we need to first find out how much of the sale falls under the 3% commission rate, and how much falls under the 5% commission rate.

The first P 2,500,000 of the sale falls under the 3% commission rate. So, 3% of P 2,500,000 is P 2,500,000 x 0.03 = P 75,000.

The remaining P 5,000,000 of the sale falls under the 5% commission rate. So, 5% of P 5,000,000 is P 5,000,000 x 0.05 = P 250,000.

Now, we add the commission earned from the 3% rate and the 5% rate to find the total commission:

P 75,000 + P 250,000 = P 325,000

Therefore, the real estate agent receives P 325,000 as commission on the sale of P 7,500,000 property.

What Is a Conditional Sales Agreement?

A conditional sales agreement is a financing arrangement where a buyer takes possession of an asset, but its title and right of repossession remain with the seller until the purchase price is paid in full.

The purchaser can take possession of the property as soon as the agreement is in force, but does not own the property until they have fully paid for it, which is usually done in installments. If the business defaults on its payments, the seller will repossess the item.

Conditional sales agreements are often put in place during the financing of machinery and equipment, as well as various forms of real estate.
