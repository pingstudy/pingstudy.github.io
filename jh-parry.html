10. what is the meaning of this poem?​ jhs po


The poem I Made My Mind a Beggar's Bowl is about one's desire for knowledge and truth. For a beggar to survive, he must reach out to other people, forage for goods, and seek a living. It is not enough for a beggar to wait for other people to approach him. The same also goes for learning: knowledge does not suddenly fall on one's lap, one must seek it out.

Knowledge is very precious which is why we must always work hard to attain it. You do not just sit quietly in one corner all day. You must listen, read, question, and understand to gain knowledge. Others even go as far as proving that something is right or wrong through experimentation and research.

In the poem's middle, the poet mentions that he stumbled on his way to his guru. His guru sees the dirt in his bowl and throws the content away. This symbolizes a person's path to seeking knowledge. There may be times when we attain knowledge but find out later on that what we know is proven to be false. If a person corrects our knowledge and belief, we must be humble enough to accept our shortcomings and be willing to adjust to learn what is right.

The poem must be taught to every student so that they know that learning does not end at school nor with a diploma. Learning is continuous, and learning must be flexible. We do not stick to what we know now but must accept that there will be a day in the future when discoveries will challenge our knowledge.

13. ETENCIES: Explain the meaning of mobile device.LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-1 Distinguish the characteristics of a mobile device. LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-2 Differentiate the type of mobile device LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-3​


Answer:

LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-1: Explain the meaning of mobile device

A mobile device refers to any portable electronic device that is designed for communication, productivity, or entertainment purposes. It is a small-sized electronic gadget that can be carried around easily and can be used to access various functions, including voice and video calls, messaging, internet browsing, multimedia playback, and running applications. Mobile devices come in different shapes and sizes, ranging from smartphones, tablets, laptops, to wearables such as smartwatches and fitness trackers. They are an essential tool for staying connected and productive in the modern world.

LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-2: Distinguish the characteristics of a mobile device

The characteristics of a mobile device include portability, wireless connectivity, touch screen interface, app support, and sensor integration. Portability is a defining feature of mobile devices, as they are designed to be carried around easily. Wireless connectivity allows mobile devices to access the internet and connect with other devices without the need for wires. Touch screen interface is a common feature of mobile devices, allowing users to interact with the device using finger gestures. App support means that mobile devices can run various applications designed for specific purposes, such as productivity, entertainment, and social media. Sensor integration, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, and GPS, allows mobile devices to perform various functions such as motion sensing, location tracking, and orientation detection.

LS6DC-DD-PSE-AE/JHS-3: Differentiate the type of mobile device

There are several types of mobile devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and wearables. Smartphones are handheld devices that offer voice calling, messaging, and internet access. They are designed to fit in the palm of your hand and are the most common type of mobile device. Tablets are larger than smartphones and are suitable for media consumption, web browsing, and productivity tasks. Laptops are larger and more powerful than smartphones and tablets, designed for intensive computing tasks such as content creation. Wearables are a new category of mobile devices that are designed to be worn on the body, such as smartwatches, fitness trackers, and augmented reality glasses. Wearables offer specialized functions such as health monitoring, activity tracking, and notifications.
