24. if 10 rubies can be exchange for 2 sapphires,7 sapphires can be exchanged for 3 emeralds, 12 emeralds can be exchanged for 2 diamonds,how many rubies can 7 diamonds be exchanged for?


Answer:

490 rubies


Step-by-step explanation:

10 rubies = 2 sapphires

7 sapphires = 3 emeralds

12 emeralds = 2 diamonds


Find the number of emeralds 7 diamonds can exchange for:

2 diamonds = 12 emeralds

1 diamond = 12 ÷ 2 = 6 emeralds

7 diamonds = 6 x 7 = 42 emeralds


Find the number of sapphires 42 emeralds can exchange for:

3 emeralds = 7 sapphires

1 emerald = 7 ÷ 3 = 7/3 sapphires

42 emeralds = 7/3 x 42 = 98 sapphires


Find the number of rubies 98 sapphires can exchange for:

2 sapphires = 10 rubies

1 sapphire = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 rubies

98 sapphires = 5 x 98 = 490 rubies


Answer: 7 diamonds can exchange for 490 rubies.

28. emerald is 5 years older than Sapphire. Ruby is twice as old as Sapphire. The sum of the three ages is 65. How old is each?


Let:         x be Sapphire's age.         2x be Ruby's age.         x+5 be Emerald's ageSolution:       x + 2x +( x+5) = 65                  4x+ 5 = 65                       4x = 65- 5                        4x = 60         Divide both sides by 4           So,  x= 15Therefore, Sapphire is 15 years old. By substitution,               Ruby is 2x= 2(15)               So, Ruby is 30 years old.                Emerald is x+5= 15+ 5               So Emerald is 20 years old.To check:             x + 2x + (x+5)= 65            15 + 2(15) + (15+ 5) = 65             15 + 30 + 20 = 65                            65= 65
