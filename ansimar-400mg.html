3. a 125ml container of Amoxicilin contains 600mg/15ml. how many ml would be use to administer 400mg of Amoxicilin? ​


Answer: Divide the 600 mg by 5 to find how many milligrams are in 1 ml. Then flip that number upside down and multiply by 400 to find out how many ml you need. 600 mg/5ml = 120 mg/ml, or 1 ml/120 mg. 400 mg X 1 ml/120 mg gives you the answer.
