15. there are 40 apples that will be given as a gift to a group of students attending a class in barangay but in the end of the ckass 1 student decided to go home and not to get 2 more apples how many students attended the classplsssssss po patulong need na po ngayon​


Step-by-step explanation:

If there were originally 40 apples to be given to the students and 1 student decided not to take 2 apples, then there must be 2 less apples given, which means 38 apples were distributed to the students who attended the class.

Let's assume that each student received one apple. To find out how many students attended the class, we can divide the total number of apples distributed by the number of apples received by each student:

Number of students = Total number of apples distributed / Number of apples received by each student

Number of students = 38 / 1

Number of students = 38

Therefore, there were 38 students who attended the class in barangay and received the apples.

Answer:

Let x be the number of students who attended the class.

If each student received 2 apples, then the total number of apples given to the students would be 2x.

However, one student did not get 2 apples, so the total number of apples given is 2x - 2.

We are given that there were 40 apples in total, so we can set up the equation:

2x - 2 = 40

Adding 2 to both sides:

2x = 42

Dividing both sides by 2:

x = 21

Therefore, there were 21 students who attended the class.
