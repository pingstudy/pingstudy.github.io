27. paki sagot james Lodi, the dimensions L,W and H of a rectangular box are changing at the rates dLdt=0.1cm/sec, dWdt=−0.2cm/sec and dHdt=0.3cm/sec, at what rate is the volume of the box changing when L=20cm, W=8cmand H=5cm?​


Answer:

volume V of rectangular box with dimensions L,W and H is given by

V=L(t)W(t)H(t)

where L(t) , W(t) and H(t) are functions of time t.

Since the dimensions of the box are changing with time, the volume of the box is also changing with time at the rate of change of V is given by the first derivative dVdt.

dVdt=W(t)H(t)dLdt+L(t)H(t)dWdt+L(t)W(t)dHdt

Substitute the known quantities by their numerical values

dVdt=8×5×0.1+20×5×(−0.2)+20×8×0.3=32cm3/sec
