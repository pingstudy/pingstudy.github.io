5. story map of the selection, Gestures by Bernice C. Roldan. Characters Main Events Setting Connflict Resolution Themes


The characters in the story are "I" and "the mute girl", the main event is in the morning, the setting is Tuesday morning, the conflict is about inner conflict of the main character, the resolution is about the acceptance of the main character, and the theme is social humanity.

The characters in the story are "I" and "the mute girl". Where the main character has a kind nature even though he likes to grumble, seen in the sentence "While grumbling around my toothbrush, I went to my bedroom to get the ten pesos money that tangled," while the mute girl has an ambitious and determined character. 

The main events in the story are about the morning atmosphere that brings the main character together with a mute girl in front of his house. 

The setting used in the story, which is Tuesday morning approaching Christmas, is shown in the sentence "Someone knocked on the door this morning, and I opened it, still brushing my teeth, automatically like a robot". Then, "So today is Gray Tuesday. I'm mute with a mouth full of mint foam. A girl with long hair is standing there". 

The conflict that occurs in the story is about the inner conflict of the main character. He is shown in the sentence, "Now that I think about it, he could be a fake. Maybe he can talk and hear as well as I can. But it doesn't matter".

The resolution in the story regarding the acceptance of the main character towards the mute girl who came to him can be seen from the editorial: "Because at that moment before I closed the door, I realized that we were the same. We said goodbye in sign language". 

The theme of this gesture story is about social humanity. It can see in the meeting between the main character and the mute girl.

11. Problem solving show your solution Alvin and Bernice went to the market to buy some vegetables. alvin bought 2.4kg of eggplant at ₱25.50 a kilogram while bernice bought 1.6kg of carrots at ₱40.50 a kilogram. Who spent less and by how much?​​​


Solution:
Alvin bought 2.4kg of eggplant at 25.50 each kg. While Bernice bought 1.6kg of carrots at 40.50 each kg. So
Alvin: (2.4) x (25.50) = 61.20
Bernice: (1.6) x (40.50) = 64.80

Therefore, Alvin spent less than Bernice by P3.60. Because (64.80 -61.20)=3.60

» Sa kapanahunang ng pamumuno ni Haring Louis XVI bago naganap ang Rebolusyong Pranses (French Revolution) ay nahahati ang mamamayan ng Pransya sa tatlong estado: First Estates, Second Estates, at Third Estates.

Sa unang estado o ang pinakamataas na estado dito ay ang mga kaparian (clergies), sila ay nagmamay-ari ng mga malalaking lupain. Sa pangalawang estado ay ang mga maharlika (nobilities), hindi sila pinababayad ng buwis at may inaakin na mga lupain. At ang panghuli ay ang ikatlong estado, mga pangkaraniwang tao (commoners), sila ay ang pinakamababang klase ng mamamayan, pinababayad ng buwis, may inaangking kaunting lupa, at karamihan sa kanila ay mga magsasaka at manggagawa na nagugutom.==============================
