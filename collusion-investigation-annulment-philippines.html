1. what is collusive oligopoly​


Answer:

One way of avoiding the uncertainty arising from oligopolistic interdependence is to enter into collusive agreements. There are two main types of collusion, cartels and price leadership. Both forms generally imply tacit (secret) agreements, since open collusive action is commonly illegal in most countries at present.


2. what is collusion means?​


Answer:

Collusion is a non-competitive, secret, and sometimes illegal agreement between rivals which attempts to disrupt the market's equilibrium. The act of collusion involves people or companies which would typically compete against one another, but who conspire to work together to gain an unfair market advantage.

Examples:

vertical price-fixing retail price maintenance.

Fixed Book Price set the price a book is sold to the public.


3. would you consider the oil companies in this case study a cartel? if there is collusion,do you think it is perfect or imperfect collusion?​


Answer:

If the companies are working together to fix prices and limit competition, it would be considered perfect collusion. If the companies are working together to control prices and limit competition, but not perfectly and some competition still exists, it would be considered an imperfect collusion.

28. ano ang ibig sabihin ng collusion?


CollusionTranslation: sabwatan

Ang salitang collusion (kolusyon) ay nangangahulugang sabwatan o pagsasabwatan ng dalawa o higit pang partido upang isagawa ang isang pandaraya o aktong makasarili na nakapipinsala sa marami.

Ang salitang collusion o pagsasabwatan ay may negatibong konotasyon at hindi ito kailanman ginagamit sa positibong konteksto.

Tignan natin ang mga sumusunod na pangungusap at kung paano ginamit ang salitang ito.

Nagkaroon ng sabwatan sa pagitan ng lokal na pulis at ng mayamang hasyendero upang gipitin ang mga manggagawa na itigil ang kanilang pag-aaklas.

Lingid sa kaalaman ng taong-bayan, may namuong pagsasabwatan sa pagitan ng korporasyon ng langis at ng gobyerno upang maitaas ang presyo ng petrolyo.

Pagkaraan ng ilang taon, natuklasan ang sabwatan sa pagitan ng dalawang kandidato sa pagka-presidente upang maibagsak ang ikatlong kandidato.
