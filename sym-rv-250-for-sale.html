The problem tells us that when we add these two numbers together and then add 12, the result is 47:

x + 4x + 12 = 47

Simplifying this equation, we can combine the like terms:

5x + 12 = 47

Subtracting 12 from both sides:

5x = 35

Dividing both sides by 5:

x = 7

So the smaller number is 7, and the larger number is 4 times this:

4x = 4(7) = 28

Therefore, the two numbers are 7 and 28.
