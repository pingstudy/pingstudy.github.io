22. FIDEL V. RAMOScharacteristics​


Answer:

Fidel Valdez Ramos GCMG (Spanish: [fiˈðel βalˈdes ra.mos]; born Fidel Ramos y Valdez; March 18, 1928),[2] popularly known as FVR and Eddie, is a retired Filipino general and politician who served as the 12th President of the Philippines from 1992 to 1998. He is the only career military officer who reached the rank of five-star general/admiral de jure who rose from second lieutenant up to commander-in-chief of the armed forces. During his six years in office, Ramos was widely credited and admired by many for revitalizing and renewing international confidence in the Philippine economy. At age 92, he is currently the oldest living former Philippine President
