20. You had to enter your email address in order to play a free game. A few days later, you started receiving multiple emails with links to other free games and downloads. You have been a victim of ______ Select one: a. Adware b. Spam c. Trojan d. Phishing


Answer:

The answer is B. Spam is electronic junk mail that people receive from unknown or unwanted sources. The content of spam is commonly some form of advertising for a product generated by electronic messaging systems in bulk. such as sending multiple emails with links to free games and other downloads.  

Here is a definition of spam: https://brainly.ph/question/1706448.

How Spam Works Spammer Harvester Spammer  

Spam is sent by a ‘spammer’ usually hired by companies or individuals to advertise a product or service. Businesses do this because sending spam is a lot cheaper than traditional means of advertising like print ads and the like.  

Harvester

A harvester is a software program designed to retrieve email addresses from online groups, websites, and others. In some cases, spammers buy email addresses from third-party apps and websites that have your email.  

Why Spam is a Problem Time Space

Waste of time

Receiving spam wastes people’s time by having them sort through spam emails just to find valuable emails from your loved ones, clients, and other important relationships.  

Waste of space

Unsolicited emails like spam take up space in your email inbox. They also consume network bandwidth that is better used with necessary information. Unfortunately, it’s nearly impossible to prevent spam. The best that email service providers can do is filter the spam before it gets to your inbox.

Here’s some information about marking emails as spam: https://brainly.ph/question/859080.

Here’s why we should avoid spamming other people: https://brainly.ph/question/1738241.
