13. We are cousins or we are both knaves. Logical Conjunction Logical Disjunction Logical Negation Logical Implication​


This statement is a Logical Disjunction.

What are Logical Connectives or Operators?

There are five Logical Connectives or Operators:

Logical Negation - a statement with a truth value that is the opposite of the truth value of the original statement, usually denoted by the use of "not"Logical Conjunction - a statement with two propositions joined by "and" or "but"Logical Disjunction - a statement with two proposition joined by "either" "or"Logical Implication - a conditional statement denoted by the use of "if" and "then"Logical Biconditional or Double Implication - a compound statement comprised of two conditional proposition and its converse

Since the statement "We are cousins or we are both knaves. " has the connective OR, then we can conclude that this is a Logical Disjunction.

The Earth is not the center of the universe.We could treat these like atomic sentences, but then we would lose a great deal of important information. For example, the first sentence tells us something about the relationship between the atomic sentences “Lincoln wins the election” and “Lincoln will be President”. And the second sentence above will, one supposes, have an interesting relationship to the sentence, “The Earth is the center of the universe”. To make these relations explicit, we will have to understand what “if…then…” and “not” mean. Thus, it would be useful if our logical language was able to express these kinds of sentences in a way that made these elements explicit. 30. Fruit Stripe Tastes great for 3 seconds Faulty logic or unsupported fact or emotional appealanswer the question and explain this.​


Answer:

The statement "Fruit Stripe tastes great for 3 seconds" is an unsupported fact. It is a subjective statement that cannot be proven or disproven. It is also a generalization that may not be true for everyone. Some people may find that the flavor lasts longer than 3 seconds, while others may not enjoy the flavor at all. Therefore, the statement should be taken with a grain of salt and not be used as a reliable source of information.
