17. Upon listening to the NCCA Hymn, how did it awaken your sense of nationalism?


Answer:

The NCCA Hymn, first of all, is beautifully written in Filipino. This in itself awakens a sense of nationalism in me because it is a reminder that we have a beautiful language and culture. While English is also a national language, there are things that are simply better to express in our own language.

Aside from that, the hymn is a work of art itself. The lyrics have been brought to life beautifully by the performance of the singers and instrument players. As they sing it, my emotions and patriotism increase too. The artistry of the piece is the best way to represent the National Commission for Culture and the Arts.

It has reminded me that if we Filipinos do not promote our culture and artists, who will? We have a rich heritage that we can all be proud of. The hymn talks about how the Filipino people are talented and artistic. It is a great way to remind us that we should be proud of what our fellow countrymen accomplish here and abroad. On top of that, it inspires me to join their ranks to represent our nation through excellence in my field in the future.
