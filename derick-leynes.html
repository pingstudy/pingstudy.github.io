25. asked GivenOperationNumber sentenceSolutionAnswerQuestion: A 25- kg bag of special Rice Cost P712.50 Mrs Leynes Buys 5kg of this rice how much will she pay?​


The operation being performed in this problem is multiplication. The number sentence that represents the problem is "25 kg * P712.50 = ?." To solve the problem, we need to first determine the cost of a single kilogram of rice by dividing the total cost of the bag by the number of kilograms it contains: P712.50 / 25 kg = P28.50/kg. We can then multiply this cost by the number of kilograms that Mrs. Leynes is buying to find the total cost: P28.50/kg * 5 kg = P142.50. Therefore, Mrs. Leynes will pay P142.50 for the 5 kg of rice. The answer to the problem is P142.50.
