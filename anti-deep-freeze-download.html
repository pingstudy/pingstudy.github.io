40% H2O 40% H2O 100% H2O 60% H2O  

the equation will look like this: .4(25) - .4x + 1x = .6(25)  

10 + .6x = 15  

.6x = 5  

x = 5 / .6  

x = 8.33  

This means that 8.33 quarts of the old needs to be drained off and replaced with 8.33 quarts of pure water.

Or

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

since we are concern with the amount of water, pure water is 100%, with 60% antifreeze 40% is water
