5. which type of volcano increases in size by the accumulation of materials erupted through the conduit on its slopes?​


Answer:

composite volcano

The essential feature of a composite volcano is a conduit system through which magma from a reservoir deep in the Earth's crust rises to the surface. The volcano is built up by the accumulation of material erupted through the conduit and increases in size as lava, cinders, ash, etc., are added to its slopes.

18. what is the trade size of intermediate mettalic conduit​


Answer:

In the AV industry, conduit is referred to by its "trade size." This may refer to inner or outer diameters of the pipe; typically, the approximate outer diameter is called "trade size." Some common types of conduit are electrical metallic tubing (EMT), intermediate metal conduit (IMC), rigid metal conduit (RMC),

Intermediate Metal Conduit (IMC) provides a lighter weight, lower-cost solution for jobs that require the same strength as galvanized rigid steel conduit. ... IMC conduit is a raceway solution which allows for future wiring changes and provides excellent mechanical protection to conductors and cables.
