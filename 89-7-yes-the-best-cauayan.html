6. PEMDAS 6+5+7-9*(8-7)/6+89


Answer: 211/2 or 105.5

Step-by-step explanation:

PEMDAS is the acronym for Parenthesis, Exponent, Multiplication, Division & Addition. Since it is arranged in such a chronological way, we just need to follow it in order to arrive with the correct answer.

= 6+5+7-9*(8-7)/6+89

= 6+5+7-9*(1)/6+89

= 6+5+7-9/6+89

= 6+5+7-3/2+89

= 11+7-3/2+89

= 18-3/2+89

= 107-3/2

= 211/2 or 105.5
