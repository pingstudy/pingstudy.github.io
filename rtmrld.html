1. Ang Paoay Church ba ay kabilang sa World Heritage Site?

✓

The Saint Augustine Church (Spanish: Iglesia de San Agustin de Paoay), also known as the Paoay Church, is a Roman Catholic church in the Philippines' municipality of Paoay, Ilocos Norte. The church, which was completed in 1710, is known for its distinctive design, which is accentuated by massive buttresses on the sides and rear of the edifice. It was designated a National Cultural Treasure by the Philippine government in 1973 and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1993 within the collective group of Philippine Baroque Churches.

2. Ang Callao Cave ba ay matatagpuan sa lalawigan ng Bohol?

x

Callao Cave is one of 300 limestone caves in the municipality of Peablanca, about 24 kilometers (15 miles) northeast of Tuguegarao City, the capital of Cagayan province, within the Peablanca Protected Landscape and Seascape in the western foothills of the Northern Sierra Madre Mountains on Luzon island in the Philippines. The name Peablanca (Spanish for "white rocks") relates to the area's abundance of white limestone rock formations. The seven-chamber display cave, first dug in 1980 by Maharlika Cuevas, is the most renowned natural tourist attraction in Cagayan province and was formally classified as an important cultural property of the Philippines in February 2020.

3. Ang Rice Terraces ba ay isang mahalagang yaman ng Pilipinas na ginawaran ng UNESCO bilang World Heritage Site?

✓

Following its acceptance of the convention on Thursday, September 19, 1985, the Philippines qualified its historical and natural sites for inclusion on the list. The Philippines had its first site featured in the list in 1993, and has had six sites on the list since 2014, encompassing nine locales. Three of the six sites are cultural, while the other three are natural. ICOMOS Philippines, a non-profit heritage group that collaborated with the UNESCO National Commission of the Philippines and the Heritage Conservation Society, started the first five sites inscribed on the UNESCO Heritage List.

4.Ang mga Lumang Bahay at Gusali sa Vigan ay mabigyan din ng titulo ng "7 Wonders of the World"

x

The Taj Mahal, the Colosseum, Chichen Itza, Machu Picchu, Christ the Redeemer, Petra, and the Great Wall of China are the current Seven Wonders of the World, as chosen by the New7Wonders Foundation. The Great Pyramid of Giza has been added to the list, albeit it is only regarded a honourable contender and not a global wonder.

5. Ang Simbahan ng Miag-ao ay matatagpuan sa lalawigan ng palawan​

x

The Miagao Church is a Roman Catholic church in Miagao, Iloilo, Philippines. It is also known as the Santo Tomás de Villanueva Parish Church. On December 11, 1993, the church was designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
