50 words essay to apply for a college scholarship

There is every essay for each intention, and scholarship essay is just one of them. It usually is mandatory in applying for a college scholarship, and the scholarship committee have a high expectation in the result of your essay writing. In college scholarship, there are several things that must be kept in mind while writing it:

Remember to align your goals in life with the college of your choice. It is even better if you include some arguments on why should this particular college is the right one for you.Most of the times, the college scholarship essay will have its own guideline, so follow them thoroughly.Include some personal information about yourself, but do not lie. Some examples may be your strengths, personal values,  as well as notable achievements and awards you have acquired.Write them way ahead of the deadline, so you won't write the essay in rush. Lastly, check for the grammar and make sure that the formatting is according to the guideline.

The essay:

I am a hard working person with a passion in [enter your major]. Also, I believe that getting this scholarship will help me in achieving my life goal. In addition, I will do my best in doing my part to the community after I graduate from [enter your university of choice].

It actually is better to make your essay as concise and clear as possible, and avoid redundant words that will fill up your word count. Also, you can always get a second opinion and make others read your college scholarship essay if you like to gain some insights and do not afraid to revise and check again and again.
