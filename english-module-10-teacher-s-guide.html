29. how will you apply the concept of monitor model as a future english teacher.​


CONCEPT OF MONITOR MODEL

As a future teacher I will apply the concept of monitor model spotting first the problem that need to be solve and after knowing the problem I will apply the 5 hypothesis in the concept monitor model. I will teach the students by translating their mother tongue language into the second language so that they will learn naturally. I will also teach language that can be easily understand and  I will  constantly monitor it if their is an error and check it as soon as possible.

THE CONCEPT MONITOR MODEL IN TEACHING LANGUAGE

As a teacher I will apply the following monitor model:

By using classroom instruction, I shall put the Acquisition or learning hypothesis to use. To ensure that students are having fun while learning language unconsciously, I will utilize play-based learning techniques.I'll also use the hypothesis model; at this point, it's important to keep track of the knowledge I taught in the acquisition hypothesis. Since the natural order theory held that the acquisition of grammatical structures happens in a predictable sequence, I will also examine the language's structure.The hypothesis from the input will also be used. To ensure that my students are learning, it implies that as a teacher, I will use terminology that is simple to understand.In affective filter hypothesis I will let the student learn language in a motivated and relaxed way.

I will apply the acquisition or unconscious learning, hypothesis model, the natural order hypothesis, input hypothesis and the affective filter hypothesis in teaching language to the students so that I can assure authentic learning.
