5. what is the purpose of cpu fan in the system unit


Answer:

It helps to cool down the CPU.

Explanation:

CPU fans are always inside the CPU. The more we use the PC, the more it heats. And as the inside of CPU gets hot, the fan sensor will alert the fan because of the temperature. Then, the fan will start to work. It's dangerous if the CPU overheats; it could destroy your PC and CPU. So it helps the system unit by cooling down the CPU whenever it gets intense heat.
