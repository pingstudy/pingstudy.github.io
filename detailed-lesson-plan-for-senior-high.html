Positive feedbacks regarding Senior High School:

Negative feedbacks regarding Senior High School:

Some students find the curriculum too challenging.Some students experience difficulty in adjusting to the new learning environment.Some students find the program too expensive.Some students find the career tracks too limited.Some students find it hard to secure work immersion or on-the-job training opportunities.

Senior High School is a crucial stage in the Philippine education system.It aims to prepare students for higher education or employment.It provides specialized tracks for students to choose from.It requires students to complete work immersion or on-the-job training.It has both positive and negative feedback from students, parents, and educators.
