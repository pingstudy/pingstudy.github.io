4. why does Emerson say that "great men" are childlike?​

Ralph Waldo Emerson believed that "great men" are childlike because they possess qualities that are similar to those of children, such as a sense of wonder and curiosity, an openness to new experiences, and a willingness to take risks. In his essay "Self-Reliance," Emerson argues that these childlike qualities are essential for greatness because they allow individuals to see the world in a fresh and creative way, unencumbered by the biases and preconceptions of others. By embracing their inner child, Emerson believed that "great men" were able to tap into a wellspring of creativity and inspiration that allowed them to achieve extraordinary things.

20. what are three ways the woods can transform a man , according to Emerson in Nature?​


Answer:

Emerson believed that spending time in the woods was good for a person's all-around wellbeing. Early on in Nature, Emerson discusses how going out into the woods and gazing at the sky can remind a person that they are one with the world and not as connected to material things as they may feel in their regular surroundings. This is something better done alone, so that the person may truly think and feel the benefits of being in nature.

The second way in which being in the woods can transform a person is that it brings a person back to the purity of childhood. A child can (usually) easily feel that connection with the universe and spirituality (not necessarily religion). Being in nature can help an adult heal from the difficulties of life the way a child would. It is important to have an open, ready mind when spending time in the woods, to allow the soul to connect readily with it.

The third way in which being in the woods can transform a person is through a spiritual connection, which is easier to attain in the aforementioned childlike state. Being in the woods allows a person to see parallels between their mind and body in nature, and gives new perspective to the things that drive them in thought and action. A person can see what is truly important to them, and the unimportant things in life fall away.

26. Explain “ The first wealth is health. – Ralph Waldo Emerson.”


"The first wealth is health" is a quote by Ralph Waldo Emerson, which means that good health is the most important asset a person can have. It suggests that having good health should be a top priority because without it, one cannot enjoy life to the fullest or achieve their goals. Good health enables people to live a fulfilling life, pursue their passions, and achieve their dreams. In essence, health is the foundation for everything we do in life. The quote emphasizes the importance of taking care of one's physical and mental health as a fundamental aspect of personal and professional success.
