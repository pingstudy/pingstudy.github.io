11. what is the cause and treatment of appendicitis?​


Cause: Appendicitis may be caused by various infections such as virus, bacteria, or parasites, in your digestive tract. Or it may happen when the tube that joins your large intestine and appendix is blocked or trapped by stool. Sometimes tumors can cause appendicitis. The appendix then becomes sore and swollen.

Treatment: Depending on your condition, your doctor's recommended treatment plan for appendicitis may include one or more of the following:
surgery to remove your appendix,
needle drainage or surgery to drain an abscess,antibiotics,pain relievers,IV fluids, and liquid diet.

17. Which model describes how data is written to a blockchain? a. Append only b. Full read and write c. Rewrite always d. Append never.


Answer:

a. Append only

Explanation:

In a blockchain, data is written to the chain in the form of blocks, and each block is added to the end of the chain in a linear, chronological order. The data in a block cannot be altered or deleted once it has been added to the chain, and new data can only be added by appending it to the end of the chain in a new block. This is known as an "append-only" model, as data can only be added to the end of the chain and cannot be modified or deleted once it has been written.

24. Appendicitis and its cause


Appendicitis can have more than one cause, and in many cases the cause is not clear. Possible causes include:

Blockage of the opening inside the appendix enlarged tissue in the wall of your appendix, caused by infection in the gastrointestinal (GI) tract or elsewhere in your body inflammatory bowel disease stool, parasites, or growths that can clog your appendiceal lumen trauma to your abdomen

Also the exact cause of appendicitis is not known, but it may be food or faeces  getting lodged in the appendix. The appendix becomes blocked, inflamed, then infected. If the infected appendix bursts, the infection is likely to spread throughout the abdomen, causing serious problems.

Other classic symptoms of appendicitis are:

nausea. vomiting. loss of appetite. constipation or diarrhea. inability to pass gas. low-grade fever and chills. a temperature between 99° and 102° Fahrenheit. stomach swelling.
