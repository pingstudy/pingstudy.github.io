3. Define the following: 1. Absolute location 2. Latitude 3. Longitude 4. Relative location 5. Vicinal location 6. Insular location


Answer:

The absolute location of a place doesn't change such as the address of a place or the latitude and longitude of a place.

Latitude is the measurement of distance north or south of the Equator. It is measured with 180 imaginary lines that form circles around the Earth east-west, parallel to the Equator. These lines are known as parallels

Longitude, is a geographic coordinate that specifies the east–west position of a point on the Earth's surface, or the surface of a celestial body.

Relative location refers to the position of a place or entity based on its location with respect to other locations.

Vicinal location refers to the location of a specific place.

insular location being, having, or reflecting a narrow provincial viewpoint

Answer:

4 units

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the y-coordinates are the same for both, Layana's house and the store, we only need to find the difference between the x-coordinates.

2 2/3 - (-1 1/3) = 2 2/3 + 1 1/3 = 4units
