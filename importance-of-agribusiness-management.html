29. how might the marketing manager of an agribusiness firm use elasticity of demand coefficients ?​


Answer:

The marketing manager of an agribusiness firm can use the elasticity of demand coefficients to make informed decisions about pricing and marketing strategies for their products.

The elasticity of demand coefficient measures the responsiveness of demand for a product to changes in price. If the coefficient is high, it means that demand is very sensitive to price changes, while a low coefficient suggests that demand is relatively insensitive to changes in price.

Using this information, the marketing manager can determine the optimal price point for their products. For example, if the elasticity of demand coefficient for a particular product is high, the manager may choose to lower the price to increase demand and gain market share. Alternatively, if the coefficient is low, the manager may choose to increase the price to maximize profits without affecting demand too much.
