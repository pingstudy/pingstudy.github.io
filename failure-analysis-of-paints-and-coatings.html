10. how many pints of paint will you buy to paint a roof, 48 m by 22 m, with two coats of paint, if a pint can cover 18 m squared?


Answer:

117.33333333333333333333333333 or 117 1/3

Step-by-step explanation:

2(48 x 22) / 18

= 2(1056) / 18

= 2112 / 18

= 117.33333333333333333333333333 or 117 1/3

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we provide the necessary information

length of roof (l) = 38 m

width of roof (w) = 22m

number of coats = 2

1 pint = 18 sq. m.

First, we solve for the area of the roof

[tex]A = l*w[/tex]

[tex]A = 48*22[/tex]

[tex]A = 1056 sq. m.[/tex]

Since the roof requires 2 coats,

2 * 1056 = 2112 sq. m.

One pint can cover 18 sq.m. of paint, therefore

[tex]\frac{1}{18}  = \frac{x}{2112}[/tex]

We solve for the value of x (which is the number of pints of paint)

[tex]18x = 2112[/tex]

[tex]\frac{18x}{18} = \frac{2112}{18}[/tex]

[tex]x = 117.33[/tex]

We need to round up to paint all of the roof

x = 118 pints

Therefore, 118 pints of paint is needed to paint the roof with two coats.

12. C. Explain why walls made with hollow blocks needs to be plastered with cement and coated with paint​


Walls made with hollow blocks need to be plastered with cement and coated with paint for several reasons. The plaster provides a smooth, even surface for the paint to adhere to. It also helps to protect the blocks from water damage and mold growth. The paint helps to protect the wall from further damage, such as UV rays and extreme weather. Additionally, the paint helps to make the wall look more attractive and can help to hide any imperfections or blemishes on the wall.

13. putting a simple coat of paint can prevent rust is it a true or false


Answer:

TRUE

Explanation:

Paint or plating(like chrome plating) prevents the H2O molecules from reaching the surface of the metal so it can't get the oxygen. As a consequence, the iron can't rust since it can't get to the water molecules when it is painted.
