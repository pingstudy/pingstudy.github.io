1. what is the message of jollibee advertisement​


Answer:

Jollibee is known for their heartwarming and family-oriented advertisements that promote their slogan "Bida ang Saya". Generally, their advertisements aim to showcase how important family values and traditions are in the Philippines and how Jollibee is a place where families can bond over delicious food and create lasting memories.

Coming from a powerful truth that we are spending time with our families more than ever, it has never happened before and may never happen again—we wanted this campaign to acknowledge the grim reality of the current pandemic but still give a positive message of hope.
