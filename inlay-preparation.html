1. describe inlaying??​


Answer: Inlay, in the visual arts, any decorative technique used to create an ornamental design, pattern, or scene by inserting or setting into a shallow or depressed ground or surface a material of a different colour or type. ... Inlay techniques are used in enamelwork, furniture decoration, lacquerwork, and metalwork.

29. Write the meaning of the following : 1. Wood turning 2. Pyrography 3. Flocking 4. Inlaying 5. Staining


1. Wood turning - is a process of using a lathe to make various forms and shapes.

2. Pyrography - is considered an art using fire or heat to decorate wood or leather.

3. Flocking - technique lines the interlor of drawers and boxes with a soft velvety finish.

4. Inlaying - combines several techniques that involve inserting pieces into a base object to incorporate new designs on the original product.

5. Staining - stains are used to color wood and give it the illusion of texture. This may come in two varieties. First, pigments based stain will color large pores of the wood, while dye based stain color small pores of the wood.
