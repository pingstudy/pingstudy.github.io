7. 2. A rectangular garden will be enclosed using 28 meters of fencing. If the area of the garden is 45 square meters, find its dimensions. Hint: P=2(L+W)​


Answer:

9mX5m

Step-by-step explanation:

Given by the fact that:

P= 2L + 2w

28m = 2L + 2W

14m = L + w

A= L x W

45= L x W

14 will be the sum of two numbers and 45 will be the product of two numbers therefore,

Let x = first number

(14 - x) = second number

(x)(14 - x)= 45

14x - x^2 = 45

-1 (-x^2 + 14x - 45 = 0) -1 --> to remove negative first term

x^2 - 14x + 45 = 0 -->factor it

(x-9)(x-5) = 0

x = 9, x = 5

Checking:

28 = 2(9) + 2(5)

28 = 18 + 10

28 = 28

45 = 9 x 5

45 = 45

Therefore, the dimensions of the garden were 9 meters in length and 5 meters in width or 9mX5m.
